# Oksana Shyshkova: A Paralympic Cross-Country Star

## Introduction
Oksana Shyshkova is a Ukrainian athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on June 10, 1991, she competes in the visually impaired (B2) classification, guided by Lada Nesterenko. Shyshkova has participated in multiple Winter Paralympic Games and has achieved significant results.

## Career Overview
Oksana Shyshkova took up skiing after her eyesight deteriorated. She has participated in various events, especially in cross-country skiing and biathlon, achieving notable results in both disciplines. 

## Major Achievements
### Cross-Country Skiing
- **2014 Sochi Winter Paralympics:** 
  - Bronze Medal in 1km Sprint Classic.
- **2018 PyeongChang Winter Paralympics:** 
  - Gold Medal in Women’s 4x2.5 km Mixed Relay.
  - Silver Medal in Women’s 15 km Free (Visually Impaired).
  - Bronze Medal in Women’s 1.5 km Sprint (Visually Impaired).
- **2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics:** 
  - Gold Medal in Women’s 15 km Classical (Visually Impaired).
  - Gold Medal in Women’s 6 km (Visually Impaired).
  - Bronze Medal in Women’s 1.5 km Sprint (Visually Impaired).

### Biathlon
- **2018 PyeongChang Winter Paralympics:**
  - Gold Medal in Women’s 10 km Visually Impaired.
  - Silver Medal in Women’s 6 km Visually Impaired.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2010 Winter Paralympics**: Events participated are not documented in this report.
- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: Cross-Country Skiing, Bronze in 1km Sprint Classic.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics:** 
  - Cross-Country Skiing: Gold in Women’s 4x2.5 km Mixed Relay, Silver in Women’s 15 km Free (Visually Impaired), Bronze in Women’s 1.5 km Sprint (Visually Impaired).
  - Biathlon: Gold in Women’s 10 km Visually Impaired, Silver in Women’s 6 km Visually Impaired.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Cross-Country Skiing, Gold in Women’s 15 km Classical (Visually Impaired), Gold in Women’s 6 km (Visually Impaired), and Bronze in Women’s 1.5 km Sprint (Visually Impaired).
