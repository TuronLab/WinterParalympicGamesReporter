# DRAHUN Serafym: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
DRAHUN Serafym is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the standing category. Born on May 22, 2005, he participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
DRAHUN's career in biathlon includes notable performances at the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships and the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Major Achievements
- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships:**
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic Standing: 14th (January 13, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Standing: 15th (January 15, 2022)
  - Men's Middle Distance Standing: 14th (January 16, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint - Classic Standing: 17th (January 22, 2022)
  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Sprint Standing: 15th (March 5, 2022)
  - Men's Long Distance Classic Standing: 15th (March 7, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Free Standing: 15th (March 9, 2022)
  - Men's Middle Distance Free Standing: 17th (March 12, 2022)

- **Overall World Cup Ranking:**
