# Ellen Westerlund: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Ellen Westerlund is a 19-year-old Swedish athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW9 classification, which is designated for athletes with impairments affecting their arms and legs, she is set to make her Paralympic debut at the 2026 Winter Paralympics in Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo. Ellen has a unilateral impairment affecting her right side, which impacts her balance and coordination.

## Career Overview
Ellen Westerlund's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by a steady progression since she transitioned to para-sports. She has been skiing since childhood.
