# GULIAEVA Irina: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Athlete

## Introduction
Irina Guliaeva is a Russian athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on May 17, 1984, she participates in the LW12 classification for sitting athletes.

## Career Overview
Irina Guliaeva's athletic career has been marked by her participation in cross-country skiing and athletics. 

- **Cross-Country Skiing:** Guliaeva has been active in cross-country skiing, particularly in the LW12 classification, where she has participated in multiple World Cup and World Championship events.
- **Athletics:** She has also competed in athletics, specifically in the T54 category.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 14th in Sprint Qualification at the World Cup, Finsterau (17 Jan 2026)
  - 14th in Sprint Final at the World Cup, Finsterau (17 Jan 2026)
  - 12th in Mass Start at the World Cup, Finsterau (15 Jan 2026)
  - 7th in Interval Start at the World Championships, Lillehammer (18 Jan 2022)
  - 6th in Interval Start at the World Cup, Canmore (5 Dec 2021)

- **Athletics:**
  - 6th in Women's 200 m T54 (Final) at the IPC Athletics World Championships (2015)
  - 5th in Women's 800 m T54 (Final) at the IPC Athletics World Championships (2015)
