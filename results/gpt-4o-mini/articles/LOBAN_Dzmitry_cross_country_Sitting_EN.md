# LOBAN Dzmitry: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing Champion

## Introduction
Dzmitry Loban is an athlete from Belarus, specializing in cross-country skiing. He competes in the LW10 classification, which is designated for athletes with physical impairments who compete in a sitting position. Loban has participated in multiple Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Dzmitry Loban's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by notable achievements across several Paralympic events. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **2010 Winter Paralympics**: Loban made his Paralympic debut in Vancouver, where he earned a bronze medal in the 4x2.5 km relay, open category.
- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: He secured two silver medals in the 15 km sitting and 7.5 km sitting events.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Loban competed in Pyeongchang but did not medal.
