**Introduction**  
Iaroslav Reshetynskyi is a visually impaired cross-country skier from Ukraine, competing primarily in the B1 or B2 classifications. He has participated in various international competitions and is preparing for the upcoming Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games. 

**Career Overview**  
Reshetynskyi's career in para sports has primarily focused on cross-country skiing and para biathlon. He initially gained recognition in cross-country events, where he has consistently earned medals at World Cup and Championship levels.
