# ZAINO Nicole: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
ZAINO Nicole is an American athlete competing in the sport of biathlon, specifically in the sitting classification. She is set to represent the United States at the 2026 Winter Paralympics in Italy, showcasing her skills in Para Nordic skiing, which includes both biathlon and cross-country skiing.

## Career Overview
Nicole's athletic career has been shaped by her participation in Para Nordic skiing, where she competes in both biathlon and cross-country skiing events. Her classification as a sitting athlete allows her to compete at a high level.

## Major Achievements
- Competing in Para Nordic skiing (Biathlon and Cross-Country) at the 2026 Winter Paralympics in Italy. [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/nicole-zaino)

## Notable Facts
