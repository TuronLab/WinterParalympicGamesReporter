# Profile of Paralympic Athlete Pawel Gil

## Introduction  
Pawel Gil is a Polish athlete competing in Biathlon and Nordic skiing within the Visually Impaired (VI) category. 

## Career Overview  
Pawel Gil has established his career primarily in Biathlon and competes in events regulated by the International Ski Federation (FIS).

## Major Achievements  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Sprint VI: 12th (2022-03-05)
  - Men's Middle Distance VI: 10th (2022-03-08)
  - Men's Individual VI: 7th (2022-03-11)
  - Men's Long Distance VI: 14th (2022-03-12)
  - Open 4x2.5km Relay: 11th (2022-03-13)

- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships:**
  - 14th in Men's Middle Distance - Classic VI.
