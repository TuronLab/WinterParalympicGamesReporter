# Iryna Bui: A Beacon of Resilience in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Iryna Vasylivna Bui is a distinguished Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the standing category. 

## Career Overview
Iryna Bui's career in biathlon has been characterized by her participation in multiple Paralympic events. She has competed in the following categories:

- **2014 Paralympic Winter Games**: Participated in biathlon events.
- **2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: Competed in the standing category across various distances.
- **2021/2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: Achieved notable success, including a gold medal.

## Major Achievements
- **Gold Medal**: Women's 10 km - Standing at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- **Bronze Medal**: Women's 7.5 km Classic - Standing at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- **4th Place**: Women's 10 km - Standing at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **4th Place**: Women's 12.5 km - Standing at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **6th Place**: Women's 6 km - Standing at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **9th Place**: Women's 7.5 km Classic - Standing at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **6th Place**: Women's 6 km - Standing at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Performance Trends
- Iryna Bui demonstrated a notable improvement from the 2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games to the 2021/2022 Games, culminating in her gold medal performance in the 10 km event.

## Paralympic Participations

## Other Sports
- Iryna Bui also competes in cross-country skiing alongside her biathlon events. 

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Iryna Bui has dedicated 12 years to rigorous training leading up to her success at the 2022 Paralympics.

## Notable Facts
- During the 2022 Winter Paralympics, Iryna Bui dedicated her medals to Ukrainian soldiers and her nation.
