# ZHAO Zhiqing: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
ZHAO Zhiqing is a Chinese athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW5/7 classification for athletes who can stand. Born on November 6, 1997, in Zhangjiakou, China, ZHAO has participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
ZHAO Zhiqing's career in biathlon includes her participation in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Major Achievements
- **Silver Medal** in the women's 12.5 km standing event at the 2022 Winter Paralympics (Time: 48:06.3) [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhao_Zhiqing).
- **Bronze Medal** in the women's 6 km standing event at the 2022 Winter Paralympics (Time: 20:05.1) [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhao_Zhiqing).
