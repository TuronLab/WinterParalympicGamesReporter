# ABE Yurika: A Profile of Perseverance and Performance in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
ABE Yurika (阿部 友里香) is a prominent Japanese athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on October 7, 1995, she competes in the LW6 classification. ABE has represented Japan in multiple Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
ABE Yurika's career in cross-country skiing began with her participation in the 2014 Sochi Paralympics. She has consistently competed in major international events, including the Paralympic Winter Games and World Championships.

### Key Participation Timeline:
- **2014**: Sochi Paralympics - Cross-Country Skiing and Biathlon
- **2015**: IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships
- **2017**: IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships
- **2018**: Pyeongchang Paralympics - Cross-Country Skiing
- **2022**: Beijing Paralympics - Cross-Country Skiing

## Major Achievements
ABE Yurika has achieved notable results in her sporting career, particularly in the following events:

- **Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - 2014 Sochi: 8th in 15km Freestyle
  - 2018 Pyeongchang: 9th in 6km Standing
  - 2022 Beijing: 8th in Long Distance Classic

- **World Championships**:
  - 2017 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships: 3rd in Women's Middle Distance Standing
  - 2015 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships: 4th in Women's Short Distance Standing

- **Biathlon**:
  - 2014 Sochi: 13th in 6km Standing Biathlon

## Paralympic Participations
- **2014 Sochi**:
  - Cross-Country Skiing: 10th in 1km Sprint Freestyle, 9th in 5km Freestyle, 8th in 15km Freestyle
  - Biathlon: 13th in 6km Standing Biathlon
- **2018 Pyeongchang**:
  - Cross-Country Skiing: 9th in 6km Standing, 11th in 10km Standing, 13th in 1.5km Sprint Classic, 13th in 7.5km Classic
- **2022 Beijing**:
  - Cross-Country Skiing: 16th in Sprint Standing, 8th in Long Distance Classic, 13th in Individual Standing
