# WANG Shiyu: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
WANG Shiyu is a Chinese athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW12 classification (Sitting). She made her mark on the international stage during the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
WANG Shiyu's career in cross-country skiing began to gain recognition with her participation in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. She also competed in Para Biathlon during the same event.

## Major Achievements
- **World Champion** in 20km interval start freestyle sitting at the 2025 FIS Para Cross-Country World Championships in Toblach, Italy.
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - 4th place in Women's Sprint Sitting Final (March 5, 2022)
  - 6th place in Women's Middle Distance Sitting Final (March 8, 2022)
  - 8th place in Women's Individual Sitting Final Round (March 11, 2022)
  - 3rd place in Women's Sprint Sitting Semifinal 1 (March 9, 2022)
  - 4th place in Women's Sprint Sitting Final (March 9, 2022)
- **Para Biathlon at Beijing 2022**:
  - 4th place in Para Biathlon.

## Performance Trends
- Achieved her first World Championship medal in 2025, winning the 20km interval start freestyle sitting race.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing
    - 4th place in Women's Sprint Sitting Final
    - 6th place in Women's Middle Distance Sitting Final
    - 8th place in Women's Individual Sitting Final Round
    - 3rd place in Women's Sprint Sitting Semifinal 1
    - 4th place in Women's Sprint Sitting Final
  - Sport: Para Biathlon
    - 4th place in Para Biathlon.

## Other Sports
- **Para Biathlon**:
  - 4th place in Para Biathlon at Beijing 2022.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
WANG Shiyu trains twice daily, focusing on consistency and discipline. Morning exercises include general stretching, and she incorporates roller skiing and strength training during the summer.

## Notable Facts
- WANG Shiyu was born on June 17, 1999, in China.
