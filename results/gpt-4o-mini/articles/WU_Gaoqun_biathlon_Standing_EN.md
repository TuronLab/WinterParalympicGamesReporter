# WU Gaoqun: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
WU Gaoqun is an athlete from the People's Republic of China, specializing in biathlon. He competes in the LW6 classification, which is designated for standing athletes. WU competed in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
WU Gaoqun's athletic journey began after an accident at the age of 16, which resulted in the loss of his right arm. This moment led him to pursue sports, particularly biathlon and cross-country skiing. He participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Sprint Standing: 6th place (March 5, 2022)
  - Men's Middle Distance Standing: 5th place (March 8, 2022)
  - Men's Individual Standing: 4th place (March 11, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Free Standing: 14th place (Qualification) (March 9, 2022)
