# DA SILVA Wellington: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
DA SILVA Wellington is a Brazilian athlete competing in the sport of biathlon. He is classified in the LW8 category (Standing) and is scheduled to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games. Wellington was born on August 4, 2006, and is currently 19 years old.

## Career Overview
Wellington's career in biathlon includes participation in various events within the Continental Cup and World Cup circuits.

## Major Achievements
- **1st Place** in Interval Start (Continental Cup, Tierra Mayor, Ushuaia, 15-08-2025)
- **1st Place** in Sprint (Continental Cup, Tierra Mayor, Ushuaia, 12-08-2025)
- **6th Place** in Sprint Final (World Cup, Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce, 28-01-2026)
