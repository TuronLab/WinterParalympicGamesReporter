# MINAMOTO Takaharu: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
MINAMOTO Takaharu is a Japanese athlete specializing in biathlon and para Nordic skiing. Competing in the LW12 classification (sitting category), he has made significant strides in his sporting career. MINAMOTO aims to represent Japan at the 2026 Milan-Cortina Paralympics.

## Career Overview
MINAMOTO's career in biathlon and para Nordic skiing includes:

- **2025**: Competed in the IBU Para-Biathlon World Championships in Slovenia and the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup in Italy.
- **2025**: Participated in the FIS Paracontinental Cup (Asia Cup) in Sapporo and the All Japan Cross-Country Ski Championships for the Disabled.

## Major Achievements
MINAMOTO has achieved notable results in both biathlon and para Nordic skiing:

### In Biathlon:
- **2025 IBU Para-Biathlon World Championships**: 13th place in Sprint 7.5km (sitting).
- **2025 IBU Para Biathlon World Cup**: 11th place in Sprint 7.5km (sitting).

### In Para Nordic Skiing:
- **2025 FIS Paracontinental Cup (Asia Cup)**: 
  - 2nd place in 5km (sitting).
  - 7th place in Sprint Combined LW12.
- **2025 All Japan Cross-Country Ski Championships for the Disabled**: 3rd place in 1km (sitting).
- **2025 All Japan Para Cross-Country Skiing Championships**: 3rd place in 2.5km LW12.
