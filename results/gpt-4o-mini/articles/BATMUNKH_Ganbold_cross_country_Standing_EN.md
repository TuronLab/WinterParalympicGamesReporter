# BATMUNKH Ganbold: A Profile of a Mongolian Cross-Country Skiing Paralympian

## Introduction
Batmönkhiin Ganbold, born on October 22, 1991, is an athlete from Mongolia, competing in cross-country skiing. He participates in the LW6 classification, which is designated for standing athletes. Ganbold has represented Mongolia in multiple Winter Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Batmunkh Ganbold's career in cross-country skiing includes:

- **2014**: Made his debut at the Sochi Winter Paralympic Games.
- **2018**: Competed in the Pyeongchang Winter Paralympic Games.
- **2022**: Participated in the Beijing Winter Paralympic Games.
- He has also been active in the World Cup circuit, achieving various rankings from 2015 to 2026.
