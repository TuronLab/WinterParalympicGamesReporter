# Guilherme Rocha: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Guilherme Rocha is a Brazilian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the sitting category (Sport Class: LW12). Born on March 20, 1996, Rocha has participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Guilherme Rocha's career in cross-country skiing includes his participation in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 19th place
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 18th place (Qualification)
  - Men's Middle Distance Sitting: 18th place
  - Mixed 4x2.5km Relay: 8th place
