# GUO Yujie: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
GUO Yujie is a prominent Chinese athlete specializing in biathlon and cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW8 category, which is designated for athletes who can stand, she has made significant strides in her sporting career. Her participation in the 2022 Winter Paralympics marked a pivotal moment in her journey.

## Career Overview
GUO Yujie's career in winter sports has been marked by her dedication and remarkable achievements. She primarily competes in biathlon, where she has gained recognition for her outstanding performances. In addition to biathlon, she also participates in cross-country skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **Gold Medal**: Women's sprint standing at the 2022 Winter Paralympics. [Source: Xinhua](https://english.news.cn/20220305/381e959102b54f1199da93b960c1d52f/c.html)
- **First Female Winter Paralympic Champion**: Achieved the title for China in an individual event at the 2022 Winter Paralympics. [Source: Global Times](https://www.globaltimes.cn/galleries/4273.html)
