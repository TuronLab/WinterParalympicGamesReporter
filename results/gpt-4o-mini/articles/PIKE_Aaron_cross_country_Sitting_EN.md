# Aaron Pike: A Versatile Paralympic Athlete

## Introduction
Aaron Pike is an athlete from the United States, specializing in cross-country skiing and wheelchair racing. Competing in the LW10 classification (sitting), Pike has participated in multiple Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Aaron Pike's athletic career began with wheelchair racing, where he competed in the Summer Paralympics before transitioning to winter sports. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **2012 Summer Paralympics**: Competed in wheelchair racing.
- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: Made his debut in cross-country skiing.
- **2016 Summer Paralympics**: Continued to compete in wheelchair racing.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Achieved results in cross-country skiing.
- **2020 Summer Paralympics**: Participated again in wheelchair racing.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Furthered his cross-country skiing career.
- **2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**: Achieved success in cross-country skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **Six-time Paralympian**:
  - 2014 Winter Paralympics: Cross-country skiing
  - 2018 Winter Paralympics: Cross-country skiing
  - 2022 Winter Paralympics: Cross-country skiing
  - 2012 Summer Paralympics: Wheelchair racing
  - 2016 Summer Paralympics: Wheelchair racing
  - 2020 Summer Paralympics: Wheelchair racing
- **2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**:
  - Gold in 12.5km seated event
  - Silver in 7.5km sprint
  - Silver in 10km event

## Performance Trends
- Demonstrated improvement in major events, particularly noted during the 2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships where he secured multiple medals.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2014 Winter Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Cross-country skiing
  - Events: 12th in 15km race, 14th in 1.1km sprint, 14th in 10km race
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Cross-country skiing
  - Events: 7th in 12.5km biathlon, 3rd in 1.1km sprint (qualifying), 6th in 15km biathlon
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Cross-country skiing
  - Event: 6th in 1.5km sprint (semi-final)

## Other Sports
- **Wheelchair Racing**:
  - 2012 Summer Paralympics: 16th in wheelchair marathon
  - 2016 Summer Paralympics: 10th in wheelchair marathon
  - 2nd at the 2022 Boston Marathon.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Aaron Pike balances his training between cross-country skiing and wheelchair racing, training six days a week. His preparation focuses on maintaining fitness across both sports, utilizing collegiate resources from the University of Illinois.

## Notable Facts
- Hometown: Park Rapids, Minnesota.
- Suffered a T-11 spinal cord injury at age 13 due to a hunting accident.
- Partner: Oksana Masters, who is also a Paralympian.
