# Profile of Wang Yue: A Paralympic Biathlete's Journey

## Introduction
Wang Yue is a prominent biathlete from China, competing in the women's category for individuals with visual impairments. She has participated in significant international competitions, including the Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Wang Yue’s career in biathlon began around 2010, and she participated in various competitions over the years, primarily focusing on sprint, individual, and relay events. Here are notable milestones in her career:

- **2010**: Began competing in biathlon events, including the European Junior Cup, IBU Cup, and World Cup.
- **2011**: Achieved several significant results in biathlon, including placements in the World Championships and World Cup events.
- **2015**: Last recorded results in biathlon competitions took place.
- **2022**: Competed in the Winter Paralympics, winning a bronze medal in the women's 10 km biathlon event.
- **2024**: Added para cross-country skiing to her repertoire with placements in international events.
