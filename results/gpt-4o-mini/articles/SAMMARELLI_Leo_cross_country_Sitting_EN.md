# Leo Sammarelli: A Rising Star in Para Nordic Skiing

## Introduction
Leo Sammarelli is a Canadian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the sit-ski category. He is set to make his Paralympic debut at the 2026 Milan Games, representing Canada in Para Nordic skiing. Leo was introduced to para Nordic skiing in 2018 through a Canadian Paralympic Committee event after suffering a spinal cord injury from a gunshot incident in 2017.

## Career Overview
Leo's athletic career has evolved significantly over the past few years. Initially a competitive boxer, he won titles such as the prestigious Inter-Regionals in Italy before his injury. Following his introduction to para Nordic skiing in 2018, he has been competing for only two years.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Bronze medal in the 5km Men's sit-ski distance race at the US Para Nordic National Championships.
  - Bronze medal in the Men's sit-ski sprint race at the US Para Nordic National Championships.

## Performance Trends
- Leo has shown a rapid progression in performance, having only competed in cross-country skiing for two years.
