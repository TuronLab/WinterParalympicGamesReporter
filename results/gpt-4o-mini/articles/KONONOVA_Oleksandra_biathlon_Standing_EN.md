# Oleksandra Kononova: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Oleksandra Mykolaivna Kononova is a distinguished Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW10 classification for standing athletes. Born on February 27, 1991, in Brovary, Ukraine, Kononova has made significant contributions to the sport, particularly in the context of the Paralympic Games and World Championships.

## Career Overview
Kononova's career in biathlon has been marked by remarkable achievements since her debut in the Paralympic arena. She has participated in both biathlon and cross-country skiing.

- **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing.
- **2014 Sochi Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing, Gold in 10 km standing.
- **2022 Beijing Paralympics**: Silver in 10 km standing, Gold in 10 km standing in cross-country skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing.
  - **2014 Sochi Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing, Gold in 10 km standing.
  - **2022 Beijing Paralympics**: Silver in 10 km standing.
  - **2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**: Gold in 6 km standing, Bronze in 10 km standing.

- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: Gold in 1 km standing, Gold in 5 km standing, Gold in 3x2.5 km relay, open.
  - **2022 Beijing Paralympics**: Gold in 10 km standing.

- **World Championships**: 5 Gold medals, 3 Silver medals, and 1 Bronze medal.

- **Overall Winner**: Biathlon and cross-country World Cup rankings in 2017.

- **Title**: 2010 Ukrainian Sports Personality of the Year.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing, Gold in 1 km standing, Gold in 5 km standing, Gold in 3x2.5 km relay, open.
- **2014 Sochi Paralympics**: Gold in 12.5 km standing, Gold in 10 km standing.
- **2022 Beijing Paralympics**: Silver in 10 km standing, Gold in 10 km standing in cross-country skiing.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Kononova trains on skates during spring and summer seasons, finding that her disability does not affect her skiing performance.

## Notable Facts
- Kononova was raised by her grandmother after being orphaned.
