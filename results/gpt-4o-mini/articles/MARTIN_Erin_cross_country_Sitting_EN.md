# Erin Martin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Erin Martin is an American athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW10 classification for sitting athletes. Born on July 18, 1986, in Seattle, Washington, Martin has participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. She works as a registered nurse at Seattle Children's Hospital.

## Career Overview
Erin Martin began training in cross-country skiing in early 2019. She qualified for the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. Martin has also participated in para biathlon, although specific achievements in this sport are not publicly available.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**
  - 15th in Women's Middle Distance Sitting (7.5km)
  - 16th in Women's Sprint Sitting (1km)
- **U.S. Sit Ski National Championships (2022)**
  - 3rd place
