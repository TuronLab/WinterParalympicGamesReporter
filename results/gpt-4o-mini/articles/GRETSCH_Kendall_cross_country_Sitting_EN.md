# Kendall Gretsch: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
Kendall Gretsch is an accomplished American athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and paratriathlon. Competing in the Sitting category, Gretsch has made significant strides in her sporting career, particularly in the context of the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Kendall Gretsch began her journey in Nordic skiing in 2016. Her career trajectory includes participation in both cross-country skiing and paratriathlon. 

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Gold Medal in Women's 6km Sitting at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympic Winter Games [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).
  - Gold Medal in Women's 12km Sitting at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympic Winter Games [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).
  - Total of 4 Gold Medals, 2 Silver Medals, and 1 Bronze Medal across various competitions, including the Paralympic Games and World Championships [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/kendall-gretsch-1078035).

- **Paratriathlon:**
  - Gold Medal in Women's Paratriathlon at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).
  - Multiple World Championships in Paratriathlon from 2014 to 2016 [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/kendall-gretsch-1078035).

## Performance Trends
- Gretsch has demonstrated consistent performance since her entry into Nordic skiing in 2016, achieving multiple medals in international competitions, including the Paralympic Games and World Championships [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/kendall-gretsch-1078035).

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 PyeongChang Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing
  - Events: Women's 6km Sitting (Gold), Women's 12km Sitting (Gold) [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).
  
- **2020 Tokyo Paralympic Games:**
  - Sport: Paratriathlon
  - Event: Women's Paratriathlon (Gold) [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).

- **2022 Beijing Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/kendall-gretsch-1078035).

- **Upcoming:**
  - Competing in the 2026 Milano Cortina Paralympic Games [Source: Paralympic.org](https://www.paralympic.org/feature/kendall-gretsch-target-milano-cortina-2026).

## Other Sports
- **Paratriathlon:**
  - Gold Medal in Women's Paratriathlon at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games [Source: American Gold Sports Alliance](https://agsa.org/2022/03/kendall-gretsch/).
  - Multiple World Championships in Paratriathlon from 2014 to 2016 [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/kendall-gretsch-1078035).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Kendall Gretsch trains at the U.S. Paralympic Training Center in Colorado Springs, Colorado [Source: YouTube](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=aNiCm3Db_fo).

## Notable Facts
