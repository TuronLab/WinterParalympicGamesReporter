# Cristian Westemaier Ribera: A Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Cristian Westemaier Ribera is a Brazilian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW11 classification for sitting athletes. Born on November 13, 2002, Cristian has made significant strides in the world of Paralympic sports.

## Career Overview
Cristian's journey in sports began as part of his rehabilitation from arthrogryposis multiplex congenita, a condition he was born with. He was introduced to Para Nordic skiing in 2015 and has since developed a competitive career in cross-country skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**
  - Silver medal in Men's Sprint - Classic Sitting
  - 6th in Men's Long Distance - Free Style Sitting
  - 8th in Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting
- **World Championships in Trondheim, Norway (March 2025)**
  - Gold medal in Men's Sprint Sitting
- **Continental Cup in Beitostolen, Norway (November 2025)**
  - Gold in both the 5km distance and 800m sprint
- **Recent Results at Finsterau World Cup (January 2026)**
  - 1st in Sprint Final
  - 1st in Mass Start
  - 9th in Mixed Relay

## Performance Trends
Cristian has achieved a total of 13 podium finishes, with 7 occurring in the 2024/2025 season.

## Paralympic Participations
- **PyeongChang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games**
  - 6th in Men's 15km Sitting
  - 15th in Men's 1.1km Sprint Sitting
  - 9th in Men's 7.5km Sitting
  - 13th in 4x2.5km Mixed Relay
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**
  - 14th in Men's Long Distance Sitting
  - 5th in Men's Sprint Sitting (Qualification)
  - 5th in Men's Sprint Sitting (Semifinal)
  - 13th in Men's Middle Distance Sitting
  - 8th in Mixed 4x2.5km Relay
- **Upcoming**
  - Preparing for the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Other Sports
Cristian has also competed in Para athletics:

  - Silver medal in Para athletics (specific event details not available).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Cristian trains with roller skis to simulate cross-country skiing and has participated in training camps in Ushuaia, Argentina, and Val di Fiemme, Italy.
