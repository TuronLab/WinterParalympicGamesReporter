# Aline dos Santos Rocha: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Aline dos Santos Rocha is a Brazilian Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the sitting category (Sport Class: LW10.5), she has represented Brazil on the international stage. 

## Career Overview
Aline's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by notable achievements. 

- **2016**: Competed in wheelchair racing at the Rio Summer Paralympics.
- **2018**: Participated in the PyeongChang Winter Paralympics, finishing 12th in the women's 5 km sitting event.
- **2022**: Competed in the Beijing Winter Paralympics, achieving 7th place in the women's long distance sitting event.
- **2023**: Achieved success at the World Para Nordic Skiing Championships, winning a gold medal in the women's sprint sitting free race and a bronze medal in the medium distance race (10 kilometers).

## Major Achievements
- **Gold Medal**: Women's sprint sitting free race at the 2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships.
- **Bronze Medal**: Medium distance race (10 kilometers) at the 2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships.
- **12th Place**: Women's 5 km sitting event at the PyeongChang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **7th Place**: Women's long distance sitting event at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **First Brazilian Woman**: To win a gold medal at the World Para Nordic Skiing Championships (2023).

## Paralympic Participations
- **2016 Summer Paralympics**: Wheelchair racing.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Cross-country skiing, 12th place in women's 5 km sitting event.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Cross-country skiing, 7th place in women's long distance sitting event.

## Other Sports
- **Wheelchair Racing**:
  - **2021**: 3rd place at the Berlin Marathon.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Aline's training regimen is tailored to enhance her performance in cross-country skiing:
- Primarily trains on a "caverboard" to mimic skiing motions.
- Engages in training sessions in Ushuaia, Argentina, and has attended training camps in Sweden and Germany.
- Focuses on strength and endurance training, with nutrition adjusted based on training intensity.

## Notable Facts
- Born on **February 20, 1991**, Aline resides in São Carlos, Brazil.
- She is married, with her husband serving as her coach.
