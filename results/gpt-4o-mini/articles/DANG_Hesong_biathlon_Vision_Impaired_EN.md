### DANG Hesong: A Profile of a Paralympic Champion

#### Introduction
DANG Hesong is an athlete from the People's Republic of China, competing in biathlon and Nordic skiing. He competes in accordance with the specific classification for visually impaired competitors.

#### Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - **Men's Sprint VI**: 8th place (Final) on March 5, 2022
  - **Men's Middle Distance VI**: 8th place (Final) on March 8, 2022
  - **Men's Individual VI**: 6th place (Final Round) on March 11, 2022
  - **Men's Middle Distance Free VI**: 8th place (Final) on March 12, 2022
  - **Open 4x2.5km Relay**: 4th place (Final) on March 13, 2022

#### Paralympic Participations
- **2022 Winter Paralympics:**
  - **Men's Sprint VI**: 8th place (March 5, 2022)
  - **Men's Middle Distance VI**: 8th place (March 8, 2022)
  - **Men's Individual VI**: 6th place (March 11, 2022)
  - **Men's Middle Distance Free VI**: 8th place (March 12, 2022)
  - **Open 4x2.5km Relay**: 4th place (March 13, 2022)

#### Upcoming Competitions
- **2026 Winter Paralympics**: Competing in Milan & Cortina d'Ampezzo, Italy from March 6 to March 15, 2026.  
- Total competitors in Para Biathlon for China: 13 men and 8 women.  
- Involved events: Individual and Sprint events for the visually impaired.
