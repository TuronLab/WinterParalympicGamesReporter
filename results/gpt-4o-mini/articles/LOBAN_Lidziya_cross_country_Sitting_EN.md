# Lidziya Loban: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country and Biathlon Athlete

## Introduction
Lidziya Loban, also known by her maiden name Hrafeyeva, is a Belarusian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW12 classification (sitting), Loban has made significant contributions to Paralympic winter sports, particularly through her participation in the Winter Paralympics and various international competitions.

## Career Overview
Lidziya Loban's career in winter sports has been marked by her participation in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. She has competed in the following events:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics** in Sochi, Russia
- **2018 Winter Paralympics** in Pyeongchang, South Korea

## Major Achievements
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Bronze medal in women's 6km sitting biathlon.
- **World Championships**:

- **European Cup**:

  - 6th in Interval Start (Vuokatti, 17-12-2021)

- **World Cup**:
