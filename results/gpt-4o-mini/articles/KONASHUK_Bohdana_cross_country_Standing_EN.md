# Bohdana Konashuk: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Bohdana Konashuk is a Ukrainian athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on March 18, 1998, she competes in the LW8 classification, which is designated for standing athletes. As an active competitor, Konashuk has participated in various international competitions, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Bohdana Konashuk's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by participation in high-level competitions. She has also competed in biathlon. Her journey includes performances in both sports, with a focus on cross-country skiing where she has achieved results.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 3rd place in Women's Sprint - Free Style Standing at the Prince George 2019 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships.
  - 5th place in Women's Long Distance - Classic Standing at the Prince George 2019 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships.
  - 6th place in World Cup Interval Start at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce, 2026.
  - 5th place in World Cup Sprint Final at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce, 2026.
  - 10th place in Women's 6km Standing at the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympic Games.
  - 7th place in Women's 10km Standing at the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympic Games.
  - 4th place in Women's Sprint Standing at the 2017 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships.

- **Biathlon:**
  - 10th place in Women's 7.5 km Classic - Standing at the Paralympic Winter Games (2021/2022 season).
