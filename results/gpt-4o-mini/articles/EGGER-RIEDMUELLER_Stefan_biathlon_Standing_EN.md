# Stefan Egger-Riedmüller: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Stefan Egger-Riedmüller is an Austrian athlete competing in biathlon and Para-Langlauf (cross-country skiing). He is set to participate in the upcoming 2026 Winter Paralympics in Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo. Born with fibular hemimelia, which affects his right calf and muscle, Egger-Riedmüller has been competing in the World Cup for only two years.

## Career Overview
Stefan Egger-Riedmüller began his competitive journey in Para-Langlauf and has recently transitioned into biathlon. Over the past two years, he has participated in various competitions, achieving significant results in the Para-Langlauf World Cup.

## Major Achievements
- **Third Place**: Overall standings in the Para-Langlauf World Cup.
- **Participation**: Scheduled to compete in the 2026 Winter Paralympics in Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo.

## Performance Trends
