# Jack Berry: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Jack Berry is an 18-year-old athlete from Missoula, Montana, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified in the LW4 category, which is designated for standing athletes. Berry is currently preparing for his debut at the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Jack Berry's athletic journey began with a focus on adaptive cycling before he transitioned to Nordic skiing. His career in cross-country skiing has seen significant milestones, including his qualification for the U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing development team in June 2024. He made his World Cup debut at the Paralympic test event in Val di Fiemme, Italy, where he finished 7th in the cross-country 10km classic.

## Major Achievements
- **Continental Cup**: 1st place in classic cross-country skiing sprint race, West Yellowstone, Montana (January 11, 2025) with a time of 3:08.69. [Source: U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing](https://www.usparanordic.org/news/2025/january/27/the-next-step-for-rising-teen-jack-berry-is-a-european-world-cup-trip)
- **U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing Development Team**: Qualified in June 2024. [Source: U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing](https://www.usparanordic.org/news/2025/january/27/the-next-step-for-rising-teen-jack-berry-is-a-european-world-cup-trip)
- **World Cup Debut**: Finished 7th in the cross-country 10km classic at the Paralympic test event in Val di Fiemme, Italy. [Source: FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=PCC&competitorid=292082&type=cups&cupcode=WC)

## Paralympic Participations
- **Milano Cortina 2026**: Aiming to compete in cross-country skiing. [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/jack-berry)

## Other Sports

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Jack Berry trains twice a day, focusing on strength training and roller skiing during the offseason. [Source: U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing](https://www.usparanordic.org/news/2025/january/27/the-next-step-for-rising-teen-jack-berry-is-a-european-world-cup-trip)

## Notable Facts
- Diagnosed with osteosarcoma at age 10, Berry underwent rotationplasty surgery and has been cancer-free since 2021. [Source: Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/jack-berry)
- Currently pursuing education at Boston College.
