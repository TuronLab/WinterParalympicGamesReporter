# WANG Chenyang: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
WANG Chenyang is a prominent Chinese athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the Men's Standing category (LW10 classification), WANG has made significant strides in the sport, particularly highlighted by his performances at the Paralympic Games. His participation in the 2018 PyeongChang and 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics has established him as a formidable competitor on the international stage.

## Career Overview
WANG Chenyang's career in cross-country skiing has seen a rapid ascent since his debut at the Paralympic level. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **PyeongChang 2018**: WANG made his Paralympic debut, participating in two events:
  - Men's 20km Free Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/pyeongchang-2018/results/cross-country/mens-20km-free-standing]
  - Men's 10km Classic Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/pyeongchang-2018/results/cross-country/mens-10km-classic-standing]

- **Beijing 2022**: WANG returned to the Paralympics with remarkable improvements, achieving podium finishes in multiple events:
  - Gold Medal in Men's Middle Distance Free Technique Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/news/china-swell-medals-tally-cross-country]
  - Silver Medal in Mixed 4x2.5km Relay [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/beijing-2022/results/cross-country/mixed-4x2-5km-relay]
  - Participated in Men's Sprint Free Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/beijing-2022/results/cross-country/men-s-sprint-free-standing]
  - Participated in Men's Middle Distance Free Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/beijing-2022/results/cross-country/men-s-middle-distance-free-standing]
  - Participated in Men's Long Distance Classic Standing [Source: https://www.paralympic.org/beijing-2022/results/cross-country/men-s-long-distance-classic-standing]
