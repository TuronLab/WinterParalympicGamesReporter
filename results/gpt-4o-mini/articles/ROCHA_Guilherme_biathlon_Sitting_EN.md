# Guilherme Rocha: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Guilherme Rocha is a Brazilian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the Sitting category. He made his mark on the international stage during the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. Rocha's participation in these events highlights his commitment to excellence in adaptive sports.

## Career Overview
Guilherme Rocha's career in winter sports has been characterized by his participation in biathlon and para cross-country skiing. He competes in the Sitting category. His journey in competitive sports culminated in his participation in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 19th place
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 18th place (Qualification)
  - Men's Middle Distance Sitting: 18th place
  - Mixed 4x2.5km Relay: 8th place
