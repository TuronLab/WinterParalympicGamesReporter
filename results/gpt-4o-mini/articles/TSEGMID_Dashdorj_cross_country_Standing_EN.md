# Tsegmid Dashdorj: A Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Tsegmid Dashdorj, born on March 19, 2001, is an athlete from Mongolia, specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW8 classification (Standing), Dashdorj has represented his country in various international competitions, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Tsegmid Dashdorj's career in cross-country skiing has seen him compete at significant international events. He participated in the 2019 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships and the 2022 Beijing Paralympic Winter Games.
