# Zhai Yuxin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Zhai Yuxin is an athlete from China, competing in the sport of biathlon. She participates in the LW10 classification, which is designated for athletes who compete in a sitting position. Zhai competed in the 2021/2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Zhai Yuxin's career in biathlon has been highlighted by her participation in the 2021/2022 Paralympic Winter Games. Competing in the sitting category, she achieved placements in her events.
