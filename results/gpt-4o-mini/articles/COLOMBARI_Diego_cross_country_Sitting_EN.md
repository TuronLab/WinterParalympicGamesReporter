# Diego Colombari: A Journey Through Paralympic Sports

## Introduction
Diego Colombari is an athlete from Italy, specializing in cross-country skiing and handcycling. He competes in the LW12 classification for sitting athletes in cross-country skiing. He is currently preparing for the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Diego Colombari's athletic career includes:

- **Handcycling**: Competed in the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.
- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Has participated in several World Cup events leading up to the 2026 Paralympics.
