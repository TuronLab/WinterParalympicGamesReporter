# A Comprehensive Profile of ABE Yurika: A Paralympic Biathlon Athlete

## Introduction
ABE Yurika (阿部 友里香) is an athlete from Japan, specializing in biathlon and cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW6 classification for standing events, ABE has participated in the Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
ABE Yurika's career in winter sports includes participation in cross-country skiing and biathlon. She has competed in two Winter Paralympics.

- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: Competed in both biathlon and cross-country skiing events.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Continued her participation in biathlon.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - 2014 Winter Paralympics: 13th place in the 6km standing biathlon ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yurika_Abe)).
  - 2018 Winter Paralympics: 13th place in the women's 7.5 km classic - standing ([source](https://www.the-sports.org/yurika-abe-biathlon-spf533179.html)).
  - Multiple top five finishes at the 2015 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships ([source](https://www.paralympic.org/news/made-homeless-tsunami-abe-pushed)).

- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - 2014 Winter Paralympics: 
    - 10th place in the 1km sprint freestyle ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yurika_Abe)).
    - 9th place in the 5km freestyle ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yurika_Abe)).
    - 8th place in the 15km freestyle ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yurika_Abe)).
