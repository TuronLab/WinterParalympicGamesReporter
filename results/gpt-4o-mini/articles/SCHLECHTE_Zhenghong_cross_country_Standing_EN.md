# Zhenghong Schlechte: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Zhenghong Schlechte is classified under the LW8 category (ST - Standing) and is currently focused on qualifying for the Winter Paralympics. 

## Career Overview
Zhenghong Schlechte began his athletic career in downhill skiing before transitioning to cross-country skiing. He has participated in various World Cup and Continental Cup events.

## Major Achievements
- **World Cup Events:**
  - 16th in Open Relay at Finsterau, Germany (January 18, 2026)
  - 27th in Sprint Final at Finsterau, Germany (January 17, 2026)
  - 27th in Sprint Qualification at Finsterau, Germany (January 17, 2026)
  - 34th in Mass Start at Finsterau, Germany (January 15, 2026)
  - 35th in Interval Start at Finsterau, Germany (January 14, 2026)
  - 19th in Mass Start at Canmore, Canada (December 7, 2025)
  - 16th in Sprint Final at Canmore, Canada (December 6, 2025)
  - 16th in Sprint Qualification at Canmore, Canada (December 6, 2025)
  - 21st in Interval Start at Canmore, Canada (December 4, 2025)
  - 9th in Interval Start at Canmore Nordic Centre, Canada (November 27, 2025)
  - 24th in Interval Start at Val di Fiemme, Italy (February 2, 2025)
  - 23rd in Sprint at Val di Fiemme, Italy (February 1, 2025)

## Performance Trends
- Active participation in World Cup events.

## Paralympic Participations
- Zhenghong Schlechte is currently aiming to qualify for the Winter Paralympics.

## Other Sports
- **Downhill Skiing:**
  - Initially participated in downhill skiing before focusing on cross-country skiing.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
- Zhenghong attended a weeklong training camp in an underground ski tunnel in Oberhof, Germany.
- He emphasizes learning from experienced athletes during competitions.

## Notable Facts
- Born on June 25, 2007, in China, Zhenghong is missing most of his left arm below the elbow.
- He is currently a senior at Mahtomedi High School in Minnesota, USA.
