# Brittany Hudak: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Brittany Hudak is a Canadian Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW6/8 classification, she has participated in multiple Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Brittany Hudak's career in cross-country skiing began with her debut at the 2014 Winter Paralympics in Sochi. Over the years, she has participated in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. Her career trajectory includes:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics, Sochi**: Competed in cross-country events.
- **2015 IPC World Cup, Asahikawa, Japan**: Achieved significant victories in cross-country.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics, PyeongChang**: Expanded her achievements to include biathlon events.
- **2021 Para-Nordic World Cup, Canmore**: Continued to excel in both cross-country and biathlon.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics, Beijing**: Further solidified her status as a top athlete in her category.

## Major Achievements
Brittany Hudak's career is marked by numerous accolades, including:

- **2014-15 World Cup Overall Cross-Country Champion**.
- **Bronze medal in 15 km classical standing** at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**.
- **Bronze medal in 12.5 km biathlon** at the **2018 Winter Paralympics**.
- **Bronze medal in 12.5 km biathlon** at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**.
- Multiple podium finishes in World Cup events, including:
  - **1st in 7.5km classic** at the **2021 Para-Nordic World Cup**.
  - **1st in sprint classic** at the **2021 Para-Nordic World Cup**.
  - **1st in 6km biathlon** at the **2021 Para-Nordic World Cup**.
  - **2nd in 10km free** at the **2021 Para-Nordic World Cup**.
  - **1st in cross-country sprint** at the **2015 IPC World Cup**.
  - **1st in cross-country long** at the **2015 IPC World Cup**.

## Performance Trends
- Consistent top finishes in World Cup events and Paralympic Games.

## Paralympic Participations
Brittany Hudak has participated in the following Paralympic Games:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics, Sochi**: 
  - 4th in team relay
  - 10th in 15km standing
  - 12th in 5km standing
  - 6th in sprint standing
- **2018 Winter Paralympics, PyeongChang**: 
  - 5th in 10km biathlon
  - 6th in sprint
  - 8th in 7.5km
  - 8th in 6km biathlon
- **2022 Winter Paralympics, Beijing**: 
  - 3rd in 15km classical standing
  - 6th in Open Relay
  - 7th in 10km free
  - 8th in 6km biathlon

## Other Sports
In addition to cross-country skiing, Brittany Hudak has also competed in biathlon, achieving the following:

- **Bronze medal in 12.5 km biathlon** at the **2018 Winter Paralympics**.
- **Bronze medal in 12.5 km biathlon** at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Brittany trains with the Sturgeon River Nordic Ski Club under the guidance of coach Brian McKeever. Her training regimen focuses on both cross-country skiing and biathlon.

## Notable Facts
- Brittany Hudak was born on June 2, 1993, in Prince Albert, Saskatchewan, Canada. She was born with a disability, missing part of her left arm below the elbow.
- In addition to her athletic pursuits, she is pursuing a degree in social work.
