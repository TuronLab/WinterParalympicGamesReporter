# Johanna Recktenwald: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction  
Johanna Recktenwald, born on June 11, 2001, in Marpingen, Saarland, Germany, is a Paralympic athlete specializing in biathlon, cross-country skiing, and para cycling. Competing in the B2 category due to her condition of cone-rod dystrophy (visual impairment), Recktenwald has competed on the international stage.

## Career Overview  
Recktenwald’s athletic journey began in her early teens, focusing on biathlon. She first participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics. Notable performances at the 2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships included multiple medal wins. She won the Overall Title of the Para-Biathlon World Cup in January 2026. Johanna is preparing for her second Paralympic appearance in the 2026 Winter Paralympics.

## Major Achievements  
- **2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**:  
  - Silver Medal - 12.5 km Individual (Biathlon)  
  - Bronze Medal - 7.5 km Sprint (Biathlon)  
  - Bronze Medal - Sprint Freestyle (Biathlon)

- **2026 Para-Biathlon World Cup**:  
  - Overall Winner  

- **Para-Sportlerin des Jahres 2025**:  
  - Acknowledged as Para Athlete of the Year  

- **World University Games 2025**:  
  - World Champion in Biathlon  
  - Silver Medal - Sprint Classical (Cross-Country Skiing)  
  - Silver Medal - 10 km Freestyle (Cross-Country Skiing)

## Performance Trends  
- Achieved 4th place in both the 10 km and 12.5 km biathlon events during the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- Competed at the World Para Nordic Skiing Championships in 2023 with three podium finishes.
- Won the overall title at the Para-Biathlon World Cup in January 2026.

## Paralympic Participations  
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**:  
  - Sport: Biathlon  
  - Events:  
    - 10 km Event: 4th place  
    - 12.5 km Event: 4th place  

- **Upcoming**:  
  - **2026 Winter Paralympics**: Competing in Italy.  

## Other Sports  
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:  
  - World University Games 2025:  
    - Silver Medal - Sprint Classical  
    - Silver Medal - 10 km Freestyle  

- **Cycling**:  
  - Active in para cycling, focusing on tandem racing events.

## Training Style / Preparation Approach  
Recktenwald trains at the para training facility in Freiburg-Schwarzwald, where she is a part of the German Paralympic team. Her training regimen is focused on improving her performance across all three sports. Additionally, she is pursuing an academic career in Health Education.

## Notable Facts  
- Johanna has been involved in community initiatives aimed at promoting inclusion in sports.  
- Her hometown of Marpingen supports her athletic endeavors.
