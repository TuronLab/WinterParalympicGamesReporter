# Benjamin Daviet: A Champion in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Benjamin Daviet is a French athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW2 classification. Born on June 16, 1989, Daviet has made a significant mark in the world of Paralympic sports, particularly through his performances in biathlon events. His journey in sports began after a life-changing accident at the age of 17, which led him to pursue a career in para sports.

## Career Overview
Daviet's career in para sports has been characterized by achievements in biathlon.

- **2014**: Made his Paralympic debut at the Sochi Winter Games, where he won a bronze medal in the 4x2.5 km relay.
- **2018**: At the PyeongChang Winter Games, he secured gold medals in both the 7.5 km standing and the 12.5 km standing events, along with silver medals in the 15 km standing and the 4x2.5 km relay.
- **2022**: Continued his success at the Beijing Winter Games, winning gold in the 12.5 km standing and the 7.5 km standing, and earning silver in the 12.5 km free standing and the 4x2.5 km relay.

In addition to biathlon, Daviet has also participated in para cross-country skiing and para rowing.

## Major Achievements
- **Paralympic Titles**:
  - 5 Gold Medals
  - 4 Silver Medals
  - 1 Bronze Medal

- **World Championship Titles**:
  - 9 Gold Medals in various events in Para Biathlon.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2014 Sochi**: Biathlon, Bronze in 4x2.5 km relay.
- **2018 PyeongChang**: 
  - Biathlon, Gold in 7.5 km standing.
  - Biathlon, Gold in 12.5 km standing.
  - Biathlon, Silver in 15 km standing.
  - Biathlon, Silver in 4x2.5 km relay.
- **2022 Beijing**: 
  - Biathlon, Gold in 12.5 km standing.
  - Biathlon, Gold in 7.5 km standing.
  - Biathlon, Silver in 12.5 km free standing.
  - Biathlon, Silver in 4x2.5 km relay.

## Other Sports
- **Para Rowing**:
  - Vice Champion of France in Para Rowing (2023).
  - Competed in Para Rowing at Paris 2024, finishing 5th in the mixed double sculls event.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Daviet engages in training sessions with the French national team for skiing and biathlon. His preparation focuses on physical conditioning and technique improvement.

## Notable Facts
- Originates from Bouchet valley, France.
- Served as the flag bearer for France at the Opening Ceremony of the Beijing 2022 Paralympics.
