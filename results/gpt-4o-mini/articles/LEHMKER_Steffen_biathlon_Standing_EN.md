# Steffen Lehmker: A Profile of Resilience and Achievement in Biathlon

## Introduction
Steffen Lehmker is a German Paralympic athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW8 classification due to plexus paralysis.

## Career Overview
Steffen Lehmker's career in biathlon includes:

- **2018**: Lehmker competed in biathlon events at the PyeongChang Paralympics.
- **2023**: He returned to the sport and achieved podium finishes at the World Championships in Östersund.

In addition to biathlon, Lehmker has a background in Nordic skiing and has participated in football and marathon running, although specific achievements in these sports are not documented.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - Bronze medal in the Mixed Relay at the 2018 Paralympics (PyeongChang).
  - Bronze medal in the Mixed Relay at the 2023 World Championships (Östersund).
  - German Champion in the 7.5 km Biathlon Sprint (January 27, 2019).

## Performance Trends
- Returned to international competition in 2023 after a nearly three-year hiatus.
- Achieved a podium finish in the 2023 World Championships.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 Paralympics (PyeongChang)**:
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events: 
    - 12.5 km - 5th place
    - Mixed Relay 4x2.5 km - 3rd place (Bronze medal)
- **2026 Paralympics (Milan-Cortina)**: Scheduled to participate.

## Other Sports
- **Nordic Skiing**: Engaged in the sport, but specific achievements are not documented.
- **Football**: Background in the sport, but no specific results available.
- **Marathon Running**: Background in the sport, but no specific results available.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Lehmker's training regimen consists of six training sessions per week. He balances his athletic commitments with his role as a vocational school teacher and family responsibilities. He primarily trains on ski rollers due to limited access to snow.

## Notable Facts
- Born on January 15, 1989, in Uelzen, Germany.
- Works as a vocational school teacher in Bad Bevensen.
- Has a two-year-old daughter.
