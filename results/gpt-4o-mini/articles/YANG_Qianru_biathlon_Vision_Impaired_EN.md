# YANG Qianru: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction  
YANG Qianru is from China, competing in biathlon and para cross-country skiing. Born on May 9, 2001, she competes in the Vision Impaired (VI) category, specifically classified as NS3.

## Career Overview  
YANG Qianru has achieved notable results at significant international competitions, most prominently the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Games. 

## Major Achievements  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Games:**
  - Women's Sprint VI: 5th place [Source: IPC]
  - Women's Middle Distance VI: 5th place [Source: IPC]
  - Women's Sprint Free VI Qualification: 6th place (in qualification round) [Source: IPC]
  - Women's Sprint Free VI Semifinal: 4th place (in semifinal) [Source: IPC]
  - Women's Individual VI Final: 5th place [Source: IPC]
- **Asian Cup:**
  - Two silver medals in previous competitions [Source: national media].

## Performance Trends  
- YANG's competitive finishes in major events consistently range between 4th and 6th place. 

## Paralympic Participations  
- **2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics:**
  - Sport: Biathlon
    - Women's Sprint VI: 5th place
    - Women's Middle Distance VI: 5th place
    - Women's Sprint Free VI Qualification: 6th place
    - Women's Sprint Free VI Semifinal: 4th place
    - Women's Individual VI Final: 5th place  

## Other Sports

## Achievements in Other Sports

## Preparation Style / Training Approach  
YANG employs a rigorous training regimen that includes multiple daily practice sessions. To build her physical strength and stamina, she consumes five eggs nightly [Source: national media].

## Notable Facts  
- YANG hails from Beijing and utilizes a guide during competitions, with Yu Hongshun serving as her guide during the Beijing 2022 events [Source: national media].
