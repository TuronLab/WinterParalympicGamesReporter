# Anaïs Delavier: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country and Biathlon

## Introduction
Anaïs Delavier is a French Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW8 classification, she has made significant strides in her sporting career. Delavier's participation in various competitions, including World Cups and Championships, highlights her dedication and skill in winter sports.

## Career Overview
Anaïs Delavier's career in winter sports has been marked by her involvement in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. Her participation in the European Youth Paralympic Games in 2020 marked a significant milestone, where she achieved notable success. Over the years, she has consistently competed in World Cup events.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 2nd place in 10 km classic at the 2024 World Para Biathlon Championships.
  - Gold medals in both short and long cross-country skiing at the 2020 European Youth Paralympic Games.
  
- **Biathlon:**
  - Gold medals in biathlon at the 2020 European Youth Paralympic Games.
  - 2nd place in Sprint Pursuit and 11th in Biathlon 12.5 km at the 2024 World Para Biathlon Championships.

## Performance Trends
- Consistent top 10 finishes in recent World Cup events.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2024 World Para Biathlon Championships:**
  - 2nd place in Sprint Pursuit (Biathlon).
  - 11th place in Biathlon 12.5 km.
  
- **2026 World Cup Events in Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce:**
  - 6th place in Mass Start (Cross-Country).
  - 9th place in Interval Start (Cross-Country) on two occasions (31-01-2026 and 29-01-2026).
  - 12th place in Sprint Final (Cross-Country).
  - 12th place in Sprint Qualification (Cross-Country).

## Other Sports
- **Biathlon:**
  - Achievements include:
    - Gold medals at the 2020 European Youth Paralympic Games.
    - 2nd place in Sprint Pursuit and 11th in Biathlon 12.5 km at the 2024 World Para Biathlon Championships.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Anaïs Delavier engages in strength training, running, and cycling as part of her preparation.

## Notable Facts
- Birthdate: 18-02-2003.
- Nationality: French.
- Club: FFH-FFS - Club des Sports La Feclaz.
