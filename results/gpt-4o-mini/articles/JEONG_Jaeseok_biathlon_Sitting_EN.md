# JEONG Jaeseok: A Profile of a Paralympic Biathlete

## Introduction
JEONG Jaeseok is an athlete from the Republic of Korea, specializing in biathlon. He competes in the Men's Sitting category.

## Career Overview
JEONG Jaeseok has participated in the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships and the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. He competes in Men's Sitting events.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 22nd (2022-03-06)
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 33rd (Qualification, 2022-03-09)
  - Men's Middle Distance Sitting: 28th (2022-03-12)
  
- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships:**
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting: 20th (2022-01-13)
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 20th (2022-01-15)
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 19th (2022-01-20)
  - Men's Sprint - Classic Sitting: 26th (2022-01-22)
