# Alexandr Gerlits: A Profile of a Paralympic Biathlete

## Introduction
Alexandr Gerlits is a male athlete from Kazakhstan, competing in the sport of biathlon. He is classified in the LW6 category, which is designated for standing athletes. Gerlits has participated in the Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Gerlits has competed in both the 2018 and 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Major Achievements
- **Bronze Medal**: Men's 12.5 km - Standing at the 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
