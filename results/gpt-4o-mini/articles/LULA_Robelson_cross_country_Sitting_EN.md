# Robelson Lula: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Robelson Lula is a Brazilian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW12 classification for sitting athletes. He competed in the 2022 Winter Paralympics held in Beijing, China.

## Career Overview
Robelson Lula's athletic journey began with swimming, followed by participation in wheelchair handball and athletics. In 2018, he transitioned to cross-country skiing. His debut at the elite level came during the 2022 Winter Paralympics, where he competed in multiple events.

## Major Achievements
- **2022 Winter Paralympics (Beijing, China)**
  - 20th in Men's Long Distance Sitting (57:17.7)
  - 21st in Men's Sprint Sitting (2:36.77)
  - 20th in Men's Middle Distance Sitting (38:20.3)
  - 8th in Mixed 4x2.5km Relay (34:10.8)
- **World Cup in Vuokatti, Finland (2018)**
  - Competed in cross-country skiing.

## Performance Trends
