# IWAMOTO Keigo: A Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
IWAMOTO Keigo is a Japanese cross-country skier born on November 20, 1995. Competing in the LW3 classification, which is designated for standing athletes, Iwamoto has participated in multiple international competitions, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
IWAMOTO's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by consistent participation in major international events. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **2014 Paralympic Winter Games**: Iwamoto began his Paralympic journey, competing in his first major international event.
- **2017 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships**: He competed in Finsterau, Germany.
- **2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: Iwamoto participated in Pyeongchang, South Korea.
- **2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: His most recent participation was in Beijing, China.
