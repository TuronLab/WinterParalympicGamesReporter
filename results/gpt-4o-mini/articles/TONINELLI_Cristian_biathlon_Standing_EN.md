# Cristian Toninelli: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Cristian Toninelli is an Italian Paralympic athlete specializing in biathlon and Nordic skiing. Born on May 20, 1988, he competes in the standing category. Toninelli has made significant strides in his sporting career since his debut in 2017.

## Career Overview
Cristian Toninelli's career in biathlon began in 2017. His journey in competitive sports has seen him participate in various events, showcasing his skills in both biathlon and Nordic skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **PyeongChang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - 17th in Men's 20km Free Standing
  - 23rd in Men's 1.5km Sprint Classic Standing
  - 21st in Men's 10km Classic Standing
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - 10th in Open 4x2.5km Relay
  - 16th in Men's Middle Distance Free Standing
  - 24th in Men's Sprint Free Standing
- **2025 World Para Snow Sports Championships:**
  - 20th in Men's 10 Km
  - 24th in Men's 20 km
- **2026 World Cup Biathlon:**
  - 13th in 7.5 km Sprint
  - 16th in Sprint Pursuit
  - 14th in 12.5 km Individual
- **Nordic Skiing:**
  - Achieved 8th place in World Cup events.

## Performance Trends
