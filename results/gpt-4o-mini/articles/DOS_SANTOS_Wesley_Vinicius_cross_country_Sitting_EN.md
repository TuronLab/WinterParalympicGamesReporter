# Wesley Vinicius DOS SANTOS: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country

## Introduction
Wesley Vinicius DOS SANTOS is a Brazilian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. He competes in the LW11.5 classification, which is designated for athletes with physical impairments competing in a sitting position. DOS SANTOS competed in the 2022 Beijing Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Wesley DOS SANTOS has participated in the 2022 Beijing Paralympic Games and several World Cup events.
