# Pavlo Bal: A Profile of a Ukrainian Paralympic Athlete

## Introduction
Pavlo Bal is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and para-biathlon. Born on June 12, 1986, Bal competes in the LW11.5 classification for sitting athletes. He is an active competitor in both winter and summer Paralympic events.

## Career Overview
Pavlo Bal's career includes participation in various competitions.

- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Competes in the LW11.5 category.
- **Para-Biathlon**: Achieved success in the 12.5 km event at the World Para Snow Sports Championships.
- **Para-Cycling**: Participated in road racing events.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - Gold medal at the 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships in biathlon (12.5 km).
  - Bronze medal in the H5 road race at the 2024 Paralympics (Para-cycling).

- **Para-Biathlon**:
  - Gold medal at the 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships (12.5 km).

- **Para-Cycling**:
  - Bronze medal in the H5 road race at the 2024 Paralympics.

## Performance Trends
- Consistent participation in FIS points lists with rankings fluctuating between 4th and 6th positions over the seasons.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Competed in cross-country skiing events.
- **2024 Summer Paralympics**: Achieved a bronze medal in the H5 road race (Para-cycling).
