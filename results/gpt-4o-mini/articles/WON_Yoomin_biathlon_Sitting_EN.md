# Yoomin Won: A Profile of a Paralympic Athlete

## Introduction
Yoomin Won is an athlete from the Republic of Korea, competing in wheelchair basketball and Para cross-country skiing. Born on January 19, 1988, he has represented his country in various international competitions, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Yoomin Won's athletic career includes participation in both summer and winter Paralympic events. His journey includes:

- **Para Cross-Country Skiing**: Competed in the Rio 2016 Paralympic Games.
- **Biathlon**: Participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
- **Wheelchair Basketball**: Engaged in various competitions.

## Major Achievements
Yoomin Won has participated in the following events:

- **Rio 2016 Paralympic Games**:
  - Competed in Nordic skiing events, achieving 1st place in Classification 11-12.
  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Competed in multiple events, securing a rank of 17th in Men's Middle Distance Sitting.

## Paralympic Participations
