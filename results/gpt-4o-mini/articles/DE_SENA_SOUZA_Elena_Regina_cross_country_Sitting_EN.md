# Elena Regina de Sena Souza: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
Elena Regina de Sena Souza is a Brazilian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the sport class LW12 (Sitting), she is scheduled to participate in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games. (Source: [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/paralympic-games/results/athlete-details/66175))

## Career Overview
Elena's career in winter sports includes participation in cross-country skiing and biathlon events. 

- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Elena has competed in various events, achieving notable placements, including a podium finish in the Continental Cup. (Source: [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/results.html?sectorcode=PCC&competitorid=290115&raceid=3753))
- **Biathlon**: She is also active in biathlon events. (Source: [International Biathlon Union](https://www.biathlonworld.com/athlete/de-sena-souza-elena-regina/55482))

## Major Achievements
