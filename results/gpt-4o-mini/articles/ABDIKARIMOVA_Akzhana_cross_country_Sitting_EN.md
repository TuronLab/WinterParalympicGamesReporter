# Akzhana Abdikarimova: A Profile of Resilience and Achievement in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Akzhana Abdikarimova is a prominent Russian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Born on April 22, 1984, she competes in the LW10.5 classification for sitting athletes.

## Career Overview
Abdikarimova's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by consistent participation in major events. She has achieved notable results in both the Paralympic Winter Games and World Championships.

## Major Achievements
- **Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - **2014 Sochi:**
    - 10th in Women's 6 km Sitting
    - 14th in Women's 12 km Sitting
    - 5th in Women's 10 km Sitting
    - 14th in Women's 1 km Sprint Sitting
    - 5th in Women's 12.5 km Sitting
    - 18th in Women's 5 km Sitting
  - **2018 Pyeongchang:**
