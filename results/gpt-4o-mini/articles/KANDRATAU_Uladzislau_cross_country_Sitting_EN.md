# KANDRATAU Uladzislau: A Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Uladzislau Kandratau is an athlete from Belarus, specializing in cross-country skiing. Born on August 5, 2003, he competes in the LW12 classification, which is designated for athletes with physical impairments competing in a sitting position. Kandratau's participation in the sport has been marked by his involvement in significant competitions, including the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships.

## Career Overview
Kandratau's career in cross-country skiing has seen him participate in various events. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **2021**: Competed in the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships, where he participated in multiple events.

## Major Achievements
Kandratau has made notable strides in his sporting career, particularly at the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships. His major achievements include:

- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**:
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting: 18th (January 13, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 21st (January 15, 2022)
  - Men's Long Distance - Free Style Sitting: 14th (January 18, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint - Classic Sitting: 19th (January 22, 2022)
