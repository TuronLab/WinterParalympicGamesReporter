# Sergey Ussoltsev: A Profile of a Paralympic Biathlete

## Introduction
Sergey Ussoltsev is a male athlete from Kazakhstan, competing in the sport of biathlon. He participates in the LW12 classification, which is designated for athletes who compete in a sitting position. Ussoltsev competed in the 2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Sergey Ussoltsev's career in biathlon has been highlighted by his participation in the 2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games. During this event, he competed in multiple biathlon events.
