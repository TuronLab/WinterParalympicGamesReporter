# Iuliia Mikheeva: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Iuliia Mikheeva is a Russian athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on January 26, 1999, she is classified under the LW8 category, which denotes standing athletes. 

## Career Overview
Iuliia Mikheeva's career in cross-country skiing includes participation in the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games. She has also competed in biathlon events. Her career trajectory includes participation in various World Championships and World Cups.
