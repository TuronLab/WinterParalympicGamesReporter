# Danielle Aravich: A Dual-Sport Paralympic Athlete

## Introduction
Danielle "Dani" Aravich is an athlete from the United States of America, competing in cross-country skiing and track and field. She is classified under the sport class LW8 (ST - Standing) and is currently an active competitor. Aravich has competed in both the Summer and Winter Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Danielle Aravich transitioned to competitive training in cross-country skiing after being identified by a U.S. Paralympic Nordic Ski coach in 2019.

## Major Achievements
- Competed in the **Tokyo 2020 Summer Paralympics**.
- Competed in the **Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics**.
- Joined the **FIS Athletes’ Commission** and **FIS Council** in 2025.

## Performance Trends
- Consistent participation in FIS events.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Tokyo 2020 Summer Paralympics**: Competed in track and field.
- **Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics**: Competed in cross-country skiing.

## Other Sports

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Danielle Aravich transitioned from volunteer work to competitive training in track and field, recognized by a U.S. Paralympic Nordic Ski coach in 2019.

## Notable Facts
- Aravich resides in Boise, Idaho. She engages in advocacy work for para athletes.
