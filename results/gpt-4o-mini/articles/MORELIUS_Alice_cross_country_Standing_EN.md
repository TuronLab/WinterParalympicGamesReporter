# Alice Morelius: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Alice Morelius is a Swedish athlete competing in cross-country skiing. She participates in the LW8 classification, which is designated for athletes with one arm impairment who compete using one pole. Morelius is set to make her Paralympic debut at the 2026 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Alice Morelius was born on January 17, 2008, in Sweden. She represents IFK Hedemora SK. Morelius achieved notable placements in World Cup events during the 2025 season.

## Major Achievements
- **2025 World Cup Events**: Achieved 8th place twice [Source: Parasport Sverige].
- **2026 Paralympics**: Selected to compete [Source: Wikipedia, Parasport Sverige].
