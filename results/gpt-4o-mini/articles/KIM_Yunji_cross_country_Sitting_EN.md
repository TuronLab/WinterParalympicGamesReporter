# KIM Yunji: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
KIM Yunji is a talented athlete from South Korea, competing in the sport of cross-country skiing. She participates in the sitting category. KIM is preparing for her upcoming participation in the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Paralympics [Source: Olympics.com].

## Career Overview
KIM Yunji's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by significant achievements. Her career trajectory includes:

- Competing in the Para Cross-Country World Cup events, where she has secured podium finishes.
- Achieving notable results in the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships.

## Major Achievements
KIM Yunji has made a remarkable impact in her sport with the following major achievements:

- **Gold Medal**: Women's Sprint Classic Sitting at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Trondheim, Norway (March 5, 2025) [Source: Alamy].
- **2nd Place**: 10km freestyle race at the Para Cross-Country World Cup in Steinkjer, Norway (March 2, 2025) [Source: FIS].
- **3rd Place**: Women's Sitting final at the Para Cross-Country World Cup in Steinkjer, Norway (March 2, 2025) [Source: FIS].
- **Title**: 2025 Sprint World Champion [Source: FIS].
- Multiple podium finishes in World Cup events, including a performance in the 2025/26 season opener in Canmore, Canada [Source: FIS].
