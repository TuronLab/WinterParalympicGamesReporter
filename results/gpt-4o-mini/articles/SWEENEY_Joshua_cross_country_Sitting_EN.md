# Joshua Sweeney: A Journey Through Paralympic Sports

## Introduction
Joshua Sweeney is an athlete from the United States, primarily competing in cross country skiing. He has also contributed to para ice hockey. Sweeney's journey in adaptive sports is marked by his transition from a U.S. Marine to a Paralympic athlete after sustaining injuries in combat.

## Career Overview
Joshua Sweeney's athletic career has evolved over the years, transitioning from para ice hockey to cross country skiing. His journey can be summarized as follows:

- **Para Ice Hockey:** Sweeney began his athletic career in para ice hockey, where he achieved notable success.
- **Cross Country Skiing:** He later shifted his focus to para-nordic skiing, participating in events such as the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
