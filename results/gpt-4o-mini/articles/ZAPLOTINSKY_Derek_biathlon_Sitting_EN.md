# Derek Zaplotinsky: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Derek Zaplotinsky is a Canadian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the men's sitting category.

## Career Overview
Derek Zaplotinsky's career in biathlon includes:

- Competing in the 2018 and 2022 Winter Paralympic Games.
- Achieving notable results in biathlon, including World Cup and World Championship events.

## Major Achievements
Derek Zaplotinsky has garnered several accolades throughout his career, including:

- **1st place** in the men's sitting category at the Para Biathlon World Cup (January 30, 2025) [Source: CBC Sports](https://www.cbc.ca/sports/paralympics/winter-sports/para-biathlon/canada-take-3-golds-para-biathlon-world-cup-1.7445477).
- **2nd place** in the men's 12.5 km individual sitting at the 2025 Para Biathlon World Championships [Source: CBC Sports](https://www.cbc.ca/sports/paralympics/winter-sports/para-biathlon/arendz-wilkie-zaplotinsky-medals-para-biathlon-world-championships-1.7454853).
- **4th place** in the biathlon sprint at the 2019 World Championships [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).
- **Crystal Globe champion** for the 2025-26 season in the men's sitting category [Source: Canadian Paralympic Committee](https://paralympic.ca/team-canada/derek-zaplotinsky).

## Performance Trends
Derek Zaplotinsky's performance includes:

- Achieving his first World Cup gold medal in biathlon on January 30, 2025 [Source: Rocky Mountain Outlook](https://www.rmoutlook.com/local-sports/biathlon-world-cup-gold-long-time-coming-for-alberta-paralympian-10183775).
- Notable improvements in his results since the 2022 Paralympic Winter Games [Source: Canadian Paralympic Committee](https://paralympic.ca/team-canada/derek-zaplotinsky).

## Paralympic Participations
Derek Zaplotinsky has participated in the following Paralympic events:

- **2018 Winter Paralympic Games**: Competed in biathlon events.
- **2022 Winter Paralympic Games**: Competed in biathlon events.

## Other Sports
In addition to biathlon, Derek Zaplotinsky has also competed in cross-country skiing, achieving the following:

- **4th place** in the cross-country middle distance at the 2019 World Championships [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Derek Zaplotinsky's training regimen includes:

- Summer training involving up to 65 km on wheels.
- Winter training with 50 km on a sit-ski [Source: The Redwater Review](https://theredwaterreview.com/2022/03/02/derek-zaplotinsky-set-to-represent-canada-at-beijing-paralympics/).

## Notable Facts
- Derek Zaplotinsky was born on April 4, 1985 [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).
- He grew up on a family farm in Edmonton, Alberta [Source: Radio-Canada](https://ici.radio-canada.ca/nouvelle/1087838/paralymiques-derek-zaplotinsky-biographie).
