# Iryna Bui: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross Country and Biathlon

## Introduction
Iryna Vasylievna Bui is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross country skiing and biathlon. Born on April 29, 1995, Bui competes in the LW8 classification, which is designated for standing athletes. Her participation in the 2022 Paralympic Winter Games held in Beijing, China, showcased her talent.

## Career Overview
Iryna Bui's career in competitive skiing began with her participation in various international events, where she has demonstrated her skills. Her journey can be outlined as follows:

- **2014 Paralympic Winter Games**: Bui made her debut at the Paralympic level.
- **2015 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships**: She achieved a bronze medal in the Women's Middle Distance Standing.
- **2017 IPC Nordic Skiing World Championships**: Bui earned two bronze medals in the Women's Sprint - Free Style Standing and Women's Long Distance Standing.
- **2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: She competed in multiple events, finishing fourth in both the Women's 10 km Standing and Women's 12.5 km Standing.
- **2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: Bui won a gold medal in the Women's 10 km Standing and a bronze medal in the Women's 7.5 km Classic Standing.
