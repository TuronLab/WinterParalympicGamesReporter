# ZHUMIK Anatolii: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country and Biathlon Athlete

## Introduction
ZHUMIK Anatolii is a 22-year-old Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW10.5 classification for sitting athletes, ZHUMIK has participated in the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Anatolii ZHUMIK began his competitive career in winter sports, focusing on both cross-country skiing and biathlon. ZHUMIK has competed in multiple events, primarily in cross-country skiing.
