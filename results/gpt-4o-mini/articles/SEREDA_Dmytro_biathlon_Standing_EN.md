# Dmytro Sereda: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Dmytro Sereda is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon and Nordic skiing. He competes in the LW8 classification, which is designated for standing athletes. Sereda is scheduled to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games. 

## Career Overview
Dmytro Sereda's career in winter sports includes participation in both biathlon and Nordic skiing. His competitive journey includes notable performances at the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon at Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships:**
  - Men's Sprint Standing: 7th place (2022-01-15)
  - Men's Middle Distance Standing: 12th place (2022-01-16)
  - Men's Long Distance - Free Style Standing: 9th place (2022-01-18)
  - Men's Long Distance Standing: 10th place (2022-01-20)
