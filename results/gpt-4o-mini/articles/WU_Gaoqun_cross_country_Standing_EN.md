# WU Gaoqun: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
WU Gaoqun is a talented athlete from the People's Republic of China, competing in the sport of cross-country skiing. He participates in the Standing category. WU competed in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
WU Gaoqun's career in cross-country skiing has been highlighted by his participation in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. Competing in the Standing category, he has shown consistent performance across various events.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's Sprint Standing: 6th place (March 5, 2022)
  - Men's Middle Distance Standing: 5th place (March 8, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Free Standing: 14th place (Qualification, March 9, 2022)
  - Men's Individual Standing: 4th place (Final Round, March 11, 2022)
