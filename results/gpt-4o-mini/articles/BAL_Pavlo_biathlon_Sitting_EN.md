# Pavlo Bal: A Journey of Resilience and Achievement in Paralympic Sports

## Introduction
Pavlo Bal is a distinguished Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon and para cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW11.5 classification (sitting), Bal has made significant strides in the world of Paralympic sports.

## Career Overview
Pavlo Bal's athletic career has been marked by resilience following a life-altering accident at the age of 30, which resulted in the loss of his legs. His journey includes participation in biathlon and para cross-country skiing, where he has achieved notable success.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - Multiple medalist at World Championships in ski racing and biathlon in both individual and team events [Source: Sokal Voice].
  - Notable performance at the 2022 World Championships, winning medals in both biathlon and cross-country skiing [Source: Sokal Voice].
  - 9th place in the 7.5 km Sprint at the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup 3, January 22, 2026 (22:44.1, 1+3 penalties) [Source: IBU].

- **Other Sports**:
  - Bronze medalist in road handbiking at the 2024 Paralympics [Source: Sokal Voice].
