**Linn Kazmaier: Rising Star of Paralympic Winter Sports**

**Introduction**  
Linn Kazmaier is a cross-country skier from Germany, born on November 5, 2006. Competing in the B3 classification for visually impaired athletes, she participated in the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.

**Career Overview**  
Linn Kazmaier participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics and is a member of the SZ Römerstein club. She competed at the 2023 World Championships.

**Major Achievements**  
- **Paralympic Achievements (2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics)**  
  - Gold Medal: 10 km Free (Cross-Country)  
  - Silver Medal: 15 km Classical (Cross-Country)  
  - Bronze Medal: 1.5 km Sprint (Cross-Country)  
  - Silver Medal: 6 km Visually Impaired (Biathlon)  
  - Silver Medal: 12.5 km Visually Impaired (Biathlon)  

- **World Championships 2023 Achievements**  
  - Gold Medal: 18 km Individual (Cross-Country)  
  - Gold Medal: 10 km Freestyle (Cross-Country)  
  - Gold Medal: 4x2.5 km Open Relay (Cross-Country)  
  - Silver Medal: Sprint Freestyle (Cross-Country)  
  - Gold Medal: 12.5 km Individual (Biathlon)  
  - Silver Medal: 7.5 km Sprint (Biathlon)  

**Paralympic Participations**
