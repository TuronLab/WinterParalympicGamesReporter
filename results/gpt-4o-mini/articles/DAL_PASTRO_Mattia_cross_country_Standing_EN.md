# Mattia Dal Pastro: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Mattia Dal Pastro is an Italian Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW6 classification (Standing), he has made significant strides in his sport. As an active participant in various competitions, Dal Pastro has established himself as a competitor in the world of Paralympic winter sports.

## Career Overview
Mattia Dal Pastro's career in sports began with hockey on wheels before he transitioned to cross-country skiing. His classification as an LW6 athlete allows him to compete against other standing athletes, and he has participated in numerous competitions.
