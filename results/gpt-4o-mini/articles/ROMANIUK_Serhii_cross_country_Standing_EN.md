# An Analytical Overview of Paralympic Athlete ROMANIUK Serhii

## Introduction
ROMANIUK Serhii is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. He competes in the LW8 classification, which is designated for standing athletes.

## Career Overview
Serhii Romaniuk's career in winter sports has been marked by participation in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. 

- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Competing in the LW8 category, Serhii has participated in several World Cup events, achieving notable rankings.
- **Biathlon**: He has also competed in biathlon.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - 5th place in the 5 km freestyle at the Soldier Hollow World Cup on March 2, 2023 (Source: [para-news.ch](https://para-news.ch/athlete/serhii-romaniuk)).
