# TAVASCI Luca: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Luca Tavasci is an Italian Paralympic athlete specializing in biathlon and cross-country skiing. He competes in the sitting classification. Tavasci made his debut at the 2026 Paralympic Winter Games in Milan Cortina.

## Career Overview
Luca Tavasci's athletic journey began after a military incident in Iraq in 2019, which resulted in the loss of his left leg. His career trajectory includes participation in various competitions, with a focus on improving his performance in both sports.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon:**
  - 12th place in the 7.5 km sprint at Notschrei (GER) 2026.
  - 8th place in the sprint pursuit at Notschrei (GER) 2026.
  - 13th place in the sprint pursuit at Szklarska Poreba Jakuszyce (POL) 2026.
  - 13th place in the 12.5 km Individual at Szklarska Poreba Jakuszyce (POL) 2026.
  - Participation in the 2026 Paralympic Winter Games in Milan Cortina.
  - Multiple top 15 finishes in World Cup events.

- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 26th place in the sprint at Val di Fiemme (ITA) 2025.
  - 17th place in the sprint at Vuokatti (FIN) 2024.
  - 24th place in the sprint at Martell (ITA).

## Performance Trends
- Notable improvement in sprint events, with consistent placements in the top 15 in World Cup events.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2026 Paralympic Winter Games, Milan Cortina:**
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events: 7.5 km sprint, sprint pursuit, 12.5 km Individual
  - Results: 12th, 8th, and 13th places respectively.

## Other Sports

  - Achievements:

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Luca Tavasci employs an extensive summer training regimen that includes handbike and ski roll activities, followed by technique refinement during World Cup events.

## Notable Facts
