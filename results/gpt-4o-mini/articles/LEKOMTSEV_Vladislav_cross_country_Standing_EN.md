# LEKOMTSEV Vladislav: A Profile of a Paralympic Champion

## Introduction
LEKOMTSEV Vladislav is a Russian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and para-biathlon. He competes in the LW6 classification, which is designated for standing athletes.

## Career Overview
Vladislav Lekomtsev's athletic career has been marked by notable achievements in cross-country skiing and para-biathlon. 

- **2014**: Competed in the Winter Paralympics in Sochi, where he won a bronze medal in the 20 km standing cross-country skiing event.
- **2021**: Achieved a gold medal in the men's 12.5 km standing cross-country skiing event at the World Para Snow Sports Championships in Lillehammer, Norway.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - Bronze medal in the 20 km standing cross-country skiing at the 2014 Winter Paralympics in Sochi. [Source: Kremlin.ru](http://en.kremlin.ru/events/president/news/20451)
  - Gold medal in the 12.5 km standing cross-country skiing event at the 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships in Lillehammer, Norway. [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladislav_Lekomtsev)

- **Para-Biathlon**:
  - Gold medal in the 6 km standing biathlon event at the 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships. [Source: Paralympic.org](https://www.paralympic.org/news/vladislav-lekomtsev-makes-it-five-out-five-final-day-para-biathlon)
