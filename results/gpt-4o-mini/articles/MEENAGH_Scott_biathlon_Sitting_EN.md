# Scott Meenagh: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Scott Meenagh is a distinguished athlete from Great Britain, competing in the sport of biathlon. He is classified under the LW12 disability class.

## Career Overview
Scott Meenagh's career in biathlon began to gain prominence following his participation in the 2018 Winter Paralympics. His journey continued with notable performances in subsequent competitions.

### Sports and Categories Participated
- **Biathlon**: LW12 classification
- **Rowing**: Competed as captain of the British Army team at the 2014 Invictus Games (Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Meenagh))

## Major Achievements
- **2023 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**: 
  - Silver medal in the 12.5 km individual event (Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Meenagh))
  - First Briton to win a medal at the World Para Nordic Skiing Championships (Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Meenagh))
  
- **World Cup in Torsby, Sweden (March 2025)**: 
  - Bronze medal in the Para-biathlon sprint (Source: [BBC Sport](https://www.bbc.com/sport/articles/c86p5710286o))

## Performance Trends
- Achieved consistent top-10 finishes in major competitions, including back-to-back top-10 finishes at the 2022 Winter Paralympics (Source: [ParalympicsGB](https://paralympics.org.uk/articles/meenagh-makes-it-back-to-back-biathlon-top-10s-i)).
- Recorded a career-best overall World Cup standing of 3rd in March 2025 (Source: [BBC Sport](https://www.bbc.com/sport/articles/c86p5710286o)).

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - 9th in the middle distance event (Source: [ParalympicsGB](https://paralympics.org.uk/articles/meenagh-makes-it-back-to-back-biathlon-top-10s-i))
  - 13th in the 12.5 km event (Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Meenagh))
  - 14th in the 15 km event (Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Meenagh))
  
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - 9th in the middle distance event (Source: [ParalympicsGB](https://paralympics.org.uk/articles/meenagh-makes-it-back-to-back-biathlon-top-10s-i))
