# Maksym Murashkovskyi: A Rising Star in Cross Country Skiing

## Introduction

Maksym Murashkovskyi is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross country skiing. Born on June 26, 2000, he competes in the NS3 classification for visually impaired athletes (VI). 

## Career Overview
