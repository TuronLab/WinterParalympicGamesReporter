# Stanislav Chokhlaev: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Stanislav Chokhlaev, a 36-year-old male athlete from Russia, is a competitor in the sport of cross-country skiing. He competes in the B1 category for visually impaired athletes. 

## Career Overview
Chokhlaev's career in cross-country skiing began during the 2014 Winter Paralympics held in Sochi, Russia. At these Games, he achieved multiple podium finishes. Chokhlaev continued to compete in subsequent years, reaching further accolades in both cross-country skiing and biathlon.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - **2014 Winter Paralympics (Sochi, Russia):**
    - Silver Medal in Men's 20 km Visually Impaired
    - Silver Medal in Men's 10 km Visually Impaired
    - Bronze Medal in Men's 15 km Visually Impaired (Biathlon)
  - **2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships (Lillehammer, Norway):**
    - Silver Medal in Men's Middle Distance - Classic VI
    - Silver Medal in Men's Long Distance - Free Style VI
  - **2022 World Para Snow Sports Championships (Lillehammer, Norway):**
    - Gold Medal in Men's Long Distance VI
    - Silver Medal in Men's Middle Distance VI
    - Silver Medal in Men's Sprint VI

- **Biathlon Achievements:**
  - **2014 Winter Paralympics (Sochi, Russia):**
    - Silver Medal in Men's 10 km Visually Impaired
  - **2022 World Para Snow Sports Championships (Lillehammer, Norway):**
    - Silver Medal in Men's 6 km Visually Impaired (Biathlon)

## Performance Trends
Chokhlaev had a successful debut at the **2014 Winter Paralympics**, achieving recognition with multiple medals. He achieved multiple podium placements during the **2021** and **2022 World Para Snow Sports Championships**. He secured a gold medal in the Long Distance event in 2022.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2014 Winter Paralympics (Sochi, Russia):**
  - Competed in Cross-Country Skiing and Biathlon, winning three medals.
- **Upcoming Participation:** 
  - **2026 Winter Paralympics** (intended participation).

## Other Sports
- **Biathlon:** 
  - Competed alongside cross-country skiing, achieving accolades including:
    - Silver Medal in Men's 6 km Visually Impaired (Biathlon) at the 2022 World Para Snow Sports Championships.
    - Silver Medal in Men's 10 km Visually Impaired at the **2014 Winter Paralympics**.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Chokhlaev trains alongside a guide, Maksim Pirogov. His coaching is led by Vyacheslav Goldinov.
