# An Analytical Overview of Paralympic Athlete ROMANIUK Serhii

## Introduction
ROMANIUK Serhii is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the Standing category. He has represented Ukraine in various international competitions, including the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Serhii Romaniuk's career in biathlon has been marked by his participation in multiple events at the Paralympic Winter Games. He competes in the Standing category, which includes a range of distances and formats. His notable performances have been primarily during the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games held in Pyeongchang, South Korea.

## Major Achievements
- **Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Men's 10 km Classic - Standing: 12th place
  - Men's 15 km - Standing: 12th place
  - Men's Sprint Classic 1.5 km - Debout: 13th place
  - Men's 12.5 km - Standing: 7th place
