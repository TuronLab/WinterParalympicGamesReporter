# Merle Menje: A Rising Star in Paralympic Sports

## Introduction
Merle Marie Menje is a talented athlete from Germany, competing in cross-country skiing and para athletics. Born on August 19, 2004, she is classified under the LW11 category for sitting athletes.

## Career Overview
Merle Menje's career began with a focus on para athletics. Her transition to cross-country skiing has also been marked by notable achievements. Below is a chronological summary of her career trajectory:

- **Para Athletics**: 
  - Competed in various events, achieving significant results at the European Championships and the Tokyo 2020 Paralympics.
  
- **Cross-Country Skiing**: 
  - Participated in the World Para Snow Sports Championships and the Para-Langlauf-WM.

## Major Achievements
Menje's accomplishments in both para athletics and cross-country skiing are impressive. Here are her major achievements:

### Para Athletics
- **2021 European Championships**:
  - 1st in 400m T54
  - 1st in 5000m T54
  - 2nd in 100m T54
  - 2nd in 800m T54
- **Tokyo 2020 Paralympics**:
  - 4th in 800m T54
  - 4th in 1500m T54
  - 6th in 5000m T54
  - 8th in 400m T54

### Cross-Country Skiing
- **2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**:
  - 5th in Mixed Relay
  - 7th in Sprint Classic Sitting
  - 9th in Middle Distance Classic Sitting
- **2025 Para-Langlauf-WM**:
  - 4th in Sprint
  - 8th in 10 km

## Paralympic Participations
- **Tokyo 2020 Summer Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Para Athletics
  - Events: 
    - 4th in 800m T54
    - 4th in 1500m T54
    - 6th in 5000m T54
    - 8th in 400m T54
- **Upcoming**: 
  - Set to participate in the Paris 2024 Summer Paralympics.

## Other Sports

  - Achievements:
    - 2021 European Championships:

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Menje engages in a training regimen that balances both athletics and cross-country skiing.
