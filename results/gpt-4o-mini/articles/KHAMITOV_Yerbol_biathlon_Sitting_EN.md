# Yerbol Khamitov: A Rising Star in Para Biathlon

## Introduction
Yerbol Khamitov is an athlete from Kazakhstan, specializing in biathlon. He competes in the sitting category and has made significant strides in his sport, particularly noted for his performances in recent international competitions. Khamitov's participation in the Paralympic Games has established him as a prominent figure in the world of para sports.

## Career Overview
Yerbol Khamitov's career in biathlon has been marked by notable achievements. He has primarily focused on the sitting category of biathlon, where he has excelled in various competitions. His career trajectory includes:

- Competing in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- Achieving significant victories in the 2025 season, including the Para Biathlon World Championships and the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup.

## Major Achievements
Yerbol Khamitov has garnered several prestigious accolades throughout his career, including:

- **Gold Medal** in the 12.5 km individual sitting event at the **2025 Para Biathlon World Championships**. [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yerbol_Khamitov)
- **Gold Medal** in the Sitting Men 7.5 km Sprint at the **IBU Para Biathlon World Cup** on March 8, 2025. [Source: Kazinform](https://qazinform.com/news/kazakhstans-yerbol-khamitov-secures-crystal-globe-at-ibu-para-biathlon-world-cup-51efe1)
- First Para Biathlon World Champion from Kazakhstan. [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yerbol_Khamitov)
- Winner of the **Crystal Globe** for the overall standings in the **IBU Para Biathlon World Cup**. [Source: Kazinform](https://qazinform.com/news/kazakhstans-yerbol-khamitov-secures-crystal-globe-at-ibu-para-biathlon-world-cup-51efe1)
