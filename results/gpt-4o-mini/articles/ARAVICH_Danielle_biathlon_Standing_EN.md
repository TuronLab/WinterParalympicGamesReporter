# The Journey of Danielle Aravich: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Danielle Aravich competes in the LW9 classification, which encompasses standing cross-country skiing and biathlon. 

## Career Overview
Danielle Aravich's career in sports began with her involvement in para athletics, where she competed in the 400m T47 event at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games. She made her debut at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games, where she participated in multiple events. 

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon:**
  - Bronze medal in the 2025 IBU Para Biathlon World Cup season climax in Torsby, Sweden.
  - Competed in four events at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:
    - 8th place in 1km Sprint Freestyle - Standing - Women.
    - 9th place in 10km Freestyle - Standing - Women.
    - 11th place in 10km Middle - Standing - Women.
    - 13th place in 6km Individual - Standing - Women.
  - Gold medal in 4 x 2.5km Relay Mixed at the 2023 World Championships.

- **Para Athletics:**
  - 10th place in 400m T47 - Women at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.

## Performance Trends
- Consistent participation in multiple biathlon events since her debut in 2022.
- Achieved her first podium finish in 2025.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games:**
  - Sport: Para Athletics
  - Event: 400m T47
  - Result: 10th place.
  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events:
    - 1km Sprint Freestyle - Standing - Women: 8th place.
    - 10km Freestyle - Standing - Women: 9th place.
    - 10km Middle - Standing - Women: 11th place.
    - 6km Individual - Standing - Women: 13th place.

## Other Sports
- **Para Athletics:**
  - 10th place in 400m T47 - Women at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Danielle Aravich employs a training regimen that emphasizes endurance, shooting consistency, and mental preparation. She incorporates horseback riding into her training to enhance her body positioning skills.

## Notable Facts
- Danielle was born without her left hand and forearm.
- She is a co-founder of Culxtured, a social media company dedicated to promoting para athletes.
- Aravich enjoys hobbies such as horseback riding, hiking, and fashion.
- She plans to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games.
