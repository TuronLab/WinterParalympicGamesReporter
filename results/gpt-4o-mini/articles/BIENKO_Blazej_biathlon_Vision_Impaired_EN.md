# Profile of Błażej Bieńko: Vision Impaired Biathlete from Poland

## Introduction  
Błażej Bieńko is a Polish athlete competing in biathlon, classified under the Vision Impaired (BI) category. 

## Career Overview  
Błażej began his athletic career with early engagements in karate and soccer during his childhood. He transitioned to biathlon after participating in training camps organized for visually impaired athletes. 

## Major Achievements  
- **6th Place** in the pursuit race at the World Championships in Canada, 2024.  
- **5th Place** in two events in Notschrei, Germany, during the 2025/2026 season.  
- **Participation** in the European Winter Para Sports Event in Poland, 2020.  

## Performance Trends  
- Błażej has shown consistent improvement, achieving positions in the top ten of various international competitions.  
- His shooting accuracy is highlighted as a significant strength in his biathlon performance.
