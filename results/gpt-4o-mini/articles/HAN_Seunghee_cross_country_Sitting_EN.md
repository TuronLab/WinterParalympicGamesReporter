# Athlete Profile: HAN Seunghee

## Introduction
HAN Seunghee is a South Korean athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and para biathlon. Competing in the LW10 classification (Sitting), she is set to participate in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
HAN Seunghee's career in winter sports has been marked by her participation in various World Cup events, primarily in cross-country skiing.
