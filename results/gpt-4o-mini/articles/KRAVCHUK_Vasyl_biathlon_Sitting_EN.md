# KRAVCHUK Vasyl: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Vasyl Kravchuk is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon and cross-country skiing. He competes in the sitting class, specifically classified as LW11.

## Career Overview
Kravchuk's career in winter sports has been marked by his dedication to biathlon and cross-country skiing.
