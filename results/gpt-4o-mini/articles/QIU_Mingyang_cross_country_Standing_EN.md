# Qiu Mingyang: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Qiu Mingyang is a Chinese athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW5/7 classification for standing athletes, Qiu has participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics, where he won a bronze medal in the 20 km classical event.

## Career Overview
Qiu Mingyang's athletic career began in athletics, where he initially excelled in track events. His journey into professional sports commenced after a coach recognized his potential following a second-place finish in a 1,500-meter race in 2002. Over the years, he transitioned through various sports:
- **Athletics**: Achieved multiple provincial gold medals in track events.
- **Cycling**: Competed at a national level before shifting focus to skiing.
- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Introduced to the sport in 2018, Qiu has achieved notable success in national competitions and the Paralympics.

## Major Achievements
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Bronze medal in the 20 km classical event.
- **National Games for People with Disabilities**: Three gold medals in cross-country skiing events.
