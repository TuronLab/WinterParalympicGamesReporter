# Nicolas Lima: A Rising Star in Cross Country Skiing

## Introduction
Nicolas Lima is an Argentine athlete specializing in cross country skiing, competing in the sitting category. Born on March 29, 1999, Lima has participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Nicolas Lima's career in cross country skiing includes:

- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**: Lima competed in three events.
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: He represented Argentina in three events.

## Major Achievements
Lima's achievements in cross country skiing include:

- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**:
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting: 22nd (January 13, 2022)
  - Men's Long Distance - Free Style Sitting: 15th (January 18, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint - Classic Sitting: 25th (January 22, 2022)

- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 17th (March 6, 2022)
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 27th (Qualification, March 9, 2022)
  - Men's Middle Distance Sitting: 23rd (March 12, 2022)

## Paralympic Participations
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
