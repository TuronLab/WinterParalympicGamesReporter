# Andrea Eskau: A Paralympic Champion in Biathlon and Beyond

## Introduction
Andrea Eskau is a distinguished German athlete who has made significant contributions to the world of Paralympic sports, particularly in biathlon and para cycling. Competing in the LW10 classification (sitting category), Eskau has showcased her exceptional talent across multiple Paralympic Games and World Championships.

## Career Overview
Andrea Eskau's athletic journey began in para cycling. Over the years, she transitioned into biathlon, where she has achieved remarkable success. Her career trajectory includes participation in both summer and winter Paralympic events.

- **2008 Beijing Summer Paralympics**: Competed in para cycling.
- **2010 Vancouver Winter Paralympics**: Made her mark in biathlon.
- **2012 London Summer Paralympics**: Continued to excel in para cycling.
- **2014 Sochi Winter Paralympics**: Achieved significant success in biathlon.
- **2016 Rio de Janeiro Summer Paralympics**: Further accomplishments in para cycling.
- **2018 Pyeongchang Winter Paralympics**: Dominated in biathlon events.
- **2020 Tokyo Summer Paralympics**: Participated in para cycling.
- **2024 Paris Summer Paralympics**: Scheduled to compete.

- **Biathlon**:
