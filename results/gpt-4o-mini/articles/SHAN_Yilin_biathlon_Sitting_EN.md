# SHAN Yilin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Shan Yilin is a Chinese athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the Sitting category. Born on April 3, 2001, she has participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Shan Yilin's career in biathlon includes her participation in the 2022 Winter Paralympics, where she achieved notable results. Competing in the Sitting category, she secured podium finishes.
