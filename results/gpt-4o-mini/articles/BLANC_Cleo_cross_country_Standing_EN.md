# Cléo Blanc: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Cléo Blanc is a French para-athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW9 classification (Standing). Born in 2008, she has been actively participating in competitive sports since 2020. Cléo is set to represent France in the upcoming Milan/Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games [Source: Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/para-cross-country-skiing-jakuszyce-feclazienne-cleo-blanc-takes-seventh-place-in-the-6-km-individual-skate-race-0129/).

## Career Overview
Cléo Blanc's career in para-athletics began in 2020. Alongside cross-country skiing, she is also engaged in biathlon [Source: Cléo Blanc's official website](https://cleo-blanc.fr/).

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 7th place in the 6 km individual skate race at Jakuszyce, Poland (2026) [Source: Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/para-cross-country-skiing-jakuszyce-feclazienne-cleo-blanc-takes-seventh-place-in-the-6-km-individual-skate-race-0129/).
  - 7th place in the World Cup Mass Start at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce (2026) [Source: FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=pcc&competitorid=292183&type=result).
  - 10th place in the Sprint Qualification at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce (2026) [Source: FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=pcc&competitorid=292183&type=result).
  - Member of the French national team for para cross-country skiing [Source: Cléo Blanc's official website](https://cleo-blanc.fr/).

## Performance Trends
- Consistent top 10 finishes in recent World Cup events [Source: Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/para-cross-country-skiing-jakuszyce-feclazienne-cleo-blanc-takes-seventh-place-in-the-6-km-individual-skate-race-0129/).

## Paralympic Participations
- **Milan/Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games**: Competing in cross-country skiing [Source: Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/para-cross-country-skiing-jakuszyce-feclazienne-cleo-blanc-takes-seventh-place-in-the-6-km-individual-skate-race-0129/).

## Other Sports

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Cléo Blanc trains at the Pôle France ski nordique, focusing on both academic and athletic development [Source: Cléo Blanc's official website](https://cleo-blanc.fr/).

## Notable Facts
- Cléo Blanc was born in 2008 and has been competing since 2020. She is a member of the French para-athlete commission [Source: Cléo Blanc's official website](https://cleo-blanc.fr/).
- She suffered a stroke in childhood, resulting in left hemiplegia, which affects her motor skills [Source: Cléo Blanc's official website](https://cleo-blanc.fr/).
