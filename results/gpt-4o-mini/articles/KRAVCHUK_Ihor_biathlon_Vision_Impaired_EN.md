# An Analytical Overview of Paralympic Athlete KRAVCHUK Ihor

## Introduction  
Ihor Kravchuk is a Ukrainian para athlete who competes primarily in biathlon and cross-country skiing. He competes in a classification for athletes with visual impairments. As of his selection for the 2026 Winter Paralympics in Milan-Cortina, Kravchuk represents Ukraine.

## Career Overview  
Kravchuk’s sporting career includes participation in para biathlon and para cross-country skiing. Key points in his career include:

- Competing in para biathlon for athletes with visual impairments, confirmed by his registration in the Ukrainian team.
- Participating in para cross-country skiing events at the 2026 Winter Paralympics.

## Major Achievements  
Kravchuk's major achievements in para sports include:

- **World Para Snow Sports Championships 2021 (Lillehammer)**:
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic VI: 10th place
  - Men's Sprint VI: 16th place
  - Men's Middle Distance VI: 16th place
  - Men's Long Distance VI: 12th place
  - Men's Sprint - Classic VI: 16th place

## Paralympic Participations  
Kravchuk is slated for the following participation in the Paralympics:

- **2026 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Sports: Biathlon and Cross-Country Skiing
  - Result: Pending (Debut)

## Other Sports  
Kravchuk also competes in para cross-country skiing:

- **Para Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - Men's Middle Distance - Classic VI: 10th place at the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships.
  - Other events included finishes ranging from 12th to 16th place in various classifications at the same championships.
