# ZHAI Yuxin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
ZHAI Yuxin is an athlete from the People's Republic of China, specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW12 classification (Sitting), Zhai participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Zhai Yuxin has participated in various competitions in winter sports, particularly in cross-country skiing events.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games:**

  - 5th in Women's Middle Distance Sitting Final (2022-03-08)

  - 9th in Women's Middle Distance Sitting Final (2022-03-12)
