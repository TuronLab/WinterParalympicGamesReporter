# JEONG Jaeseok: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Athlete

## Introduction
JEONG Jaeseok is a South Korean athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. He competes in the LW10.5 classification, which is designated for sitting athletes. JEONG has participated in significant international competitions, including the Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
JEONG Jaeseok's career in cross-country skiing has seen him compete in various prestigious events. His journey includes participation in the following competitions:
- **2021 Lillehammer World Para Snow Sports Championships**: Competed in both the Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting and Men's Sprint Sitting events.
- **2022 Beijing Paralympic Winter Games**: Competed in the Men's Sprint Sitting and Men's Long Distance Sitting events.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: 
  - 33rd in Men's Sprint Sitting Qualification (Time: 2:55.85) [Source: Korea JoongAng Daily]
  - 22nd in Men's Long Distance Sitting [Source: Paralympic.org]
  
- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**:
  - 20th in Men's Middle Distance - Classic Sitting [Source: Paralympic.org]
  - 19th in Men's Sprint Sitting [Source: Paralympic.org]
