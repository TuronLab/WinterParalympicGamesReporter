# Michael Kneeland: Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Michael Kneeland is a 20-year-old athlete from Bozeman, Montana, competing in biathlon and Para Nordic skiing. He is classified in the LW11.5 category, which is designated for sitting athletes. Kneeland is preparing for his first appearance at the Winter Paralympics, scheduled for 2026 in Milano Cortina.

## Career Overview
Michael Kneeland began his skiing journey at the age of 17. He is currently attending Montana State University, majoring in electrical engineering.
