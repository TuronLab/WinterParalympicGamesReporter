# KIM Yunji: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
KIM Yunji is a South Korean athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW10.5 classification. Born on January 16, 2000, she is recognized as a medal contender for the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
KIM Yunji represented South Korea in handball at the 2020 Summer Olympics, where her team finished in 8th place. She has achieved notable success in biathlon, including podium finishes in various competitions and national events.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - Multiple podium finishes, including 2nd place in various competitions.
  - Four titles at the National Winter Para Sports Competition in Pyeongchang.
  - Recognized as a medal contender for the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Paralympics.

- **Handball**:
  - Competed in the 2020 Summer Olympics, finishing 8th with the South Korean team.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2026 Winter Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Event: Competing as a medal contender.

## Other Sports
- **Handball**:
  - Competed in the 2020 Summer Olympics, representing South Korea and finishing 8th.

## Notable Facts
- KIM Yunji represents Wonderful Samcheok, a city in South Korea.
