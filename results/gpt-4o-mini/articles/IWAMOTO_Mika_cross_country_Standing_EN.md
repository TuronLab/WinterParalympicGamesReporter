# IWAMOTO Mika: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
IWAMOTO Mika is an athlete from Japan, competing in cross-country skiing. She is classified under the LW8 category, which is designated for standing athletes. Mika participated in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Mika's career in cross-country skiing includes:

- Competing in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
- Participating in multiple events in the Far East Cup series.

## Major Achievements
Mika has notable results in her sporting career, including:

- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - 15th place in Women's Sprint Free Standing (March 9, 2022)
  - 15th place in Women's Middle Distance Free Standing (March 12, 2022)
- **Far East Cup Events**:
  - 40th place in 10 km F at Sapporo Shirahatayama (March 4, 2023)
  - 96th place in 5 km C at Otoineppu (December 26, 2023)
  - 97th place in 5 km F at Otoineppu (December 27, 2023)
