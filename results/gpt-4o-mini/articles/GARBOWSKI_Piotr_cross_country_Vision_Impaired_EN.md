# Piotr Garbowski: A Profile of a Passionate Paralympic Athlete

## Introduction
Piotr Garbowski is a Polish cross-country skier, classified under the NS3 category for visually impaired athletes (VI). Born on September 14, 1973, Garbowski has represented Poland in both Paralympic and international cross-country skiing competitions.

## Career Overview
