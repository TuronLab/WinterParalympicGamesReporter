# Sato Keiichi: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing Athlete

## Introduction
Sato Keiichi is an athlete from Japan, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the LW8 category, which is designated for standing athletes. Sato is preparing for his sixth appearance at the Paralympic Winter Games, specifically at the upcoming Milan Cortina 2026.

## Career Overview
Sato Keiichi's career in cross-country skiing includes participation in national and international competitions.
