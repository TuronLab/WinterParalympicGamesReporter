# Bohdana Konashuk: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Bohdana Konashuk is a Ukrainian Paralympic athlete specializing in biathlon. Competing in the standing category, she has participated in various events, including the 10 km, 12.5 km, 15 km Free, 6 km, 7.5 km Classic, and Sprint Classic 1.5 km. 

## Career Overview
Bohdana Konashuk's career in biathlon has been marked by her participation in two consecutive Paralympic Winter Games:

- **2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: This was her first appearance at the Paralympics, where she competed in multiple events.
- **2021/2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: Konashuk returned to the Paralympics, competing in several events and achieving notable placements.

## Major Achievements
Bohdana Konashuk has achieved several notable placements in her biathlon career:

- **2021/2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - 10th place in Women's 7.5 km Classic - Standing (12/03/2022)
  - 10th place in Women's 12.5 km - Standing (11/03/2022)
  - 7th place in Women's 10 km - Standing (08/03/2022)
  - 10th place in Women's 6 km - Standing (05/03/2022)

- **2017/2018 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - 11th place in Women's 7.5 km Classic - Standing (17/03/2018)
  - 5th place in Women's 12.5 km - Standing (16/03/2018)
  - 8th place in Women's Sprint Classic 1.5 km - Standing (14/03/2018)
  - 7th place in Women's 10 km - Standing (13/03/2018)
  - 8th place in Women's 15 km Free - Standing (12/03/2018)
  - 10th place in Women's 6 km - Standing (10/03/2018)
