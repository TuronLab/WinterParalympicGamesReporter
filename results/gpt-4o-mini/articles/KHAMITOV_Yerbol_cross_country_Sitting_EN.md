# Khamitov Yerbol: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
Khamitov Yerbol is a 27-year-old athlete from the North Kazakhstan region, representing Kazakhstan in the sport of cross-country skiing and para-biathlon. Competing in the sitting classification (LW12), Yerbol has participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Khamitov's career in winter sports includes:

- Competing in cross-country skiing events at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- Achieving a gold medal at the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
  - 7th place in the 18 km sitting event (49:23.1).
  - 9th place in the 10 km sitting event (34:45.5).
  - 15th place in the 1.5 km sprint qualification (2:28.67), did not qualify for finals.
  - 5th place in the 4 x 2.5 km open relay with teammate Alexandr Gerlits (30:00.4).

- **Para-Biathlon:**
  - Gold medal in the Sitting Men 7.5 km Sprint at the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup.
  - Secured the Crystal Globe at the IBU Para Biathlon World Cup, becoming the first Kazakhstani athlete to win the Total Score.
