# WU Junbao: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
WU Junbao is an athlete from China, competing in the sport of biathlon and classified in the standing category. He has participated in the PyeongChang 2018 and Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
WU Junbao's career in winter sports includes participation in biathlon and para cross-country skiing. He debuted at the PyeongChang 2018 Winter Paralympics, where he competed in the men's 7.5km standing event. He participated in the Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics in the men's middle distance standing event.

## Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics**: Finished 4th in the men's middle distance standing event. (Source: Laitimes)
- **PyeongChang 2018 Winter Paralympics**: Achieved 8th place in the men's 7.5km standing event. (Source: People's Daily Online)
