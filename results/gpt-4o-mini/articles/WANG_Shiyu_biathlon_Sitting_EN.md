# WANG Shiyu: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
WANG Shiyu is a 26-year-old athlete from China, competing in the sport of biathlon and para cross-country skiing. She participates in the sitting classification. WANG made her Paralympic debut at the Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics, where she achieved commendable results.

## Career Overview
WANG Shiyu's career in winter sports has been marked by her participation in both biathlon and para cross-country skiing. Her trajectory began with her debut at the Beijing 2022 Paralympics, where she competed in both disciplines. Following her Paralympic experience, she achieved her first major international success at the 2025 FIS Para Cross-Country World Championships.

## Major Achievements
- **World Champion** in the 20km interval start freestyle sitting race at the 2025 FIS Para Cross-Country World Championships.
- **4th place** in the women's sprint sitting event at the Beijing 2022 Paralympics (Para cross-country skiing).
- **4th place** in both Para cross-country skiing and Para biathlon at the Beijing 2022 Paralympics.

## Performance Trends
- Competed in Para biathlon at the Beijing 2022 Paralympics, achieving a 4th place finish.
- Achieved her maiden World Championship medal in 2025.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympics**
  - Sport: Para cross-country skiing
  - Event: Women's sprint sitting
  - Result: 4th place
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympics**
  - Sport: Para biathlon
  - Result: 4th place

## Other Sports
- **Para cross-country skiing**
  - **4th place** in the women's sprint sitting event at the Beijing 2022 Paralympics.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
WANG Shiyu's training regimen is characterized by early starts, beginning at 05:45. Her routine includes:
- General stretching exercises.
- Focus on roller skiing and strength training during the summer months.
- Emphasis on consistency and discipline.
