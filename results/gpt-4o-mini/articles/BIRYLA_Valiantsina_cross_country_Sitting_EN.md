# BIRYLA Valiantsina: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Athlete

## Introduction
Valiantsina Biryla is a Belarusian athlete competing in cross-country skiing. Born on November 1, 1994, she participates in the LW10.5 classification, which is designated for athletes with a physical impairment who compete in a sitting position. Biryla has represented her country in multiple Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Biryla's career in cross-country skiing includes:

- Competing in the 2014, 2018, and 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- Regular participation in World Cup events.

## Major Achievements
Biryla's results in cross-country skiing include:

- **Finsterau, 2026:**
  - 11th in Mixed Relay
  - 9th in Sprint Qualification
  - 11th in Sprint Final
  - 7th in Mass Start
  - 11th in Interval Start

## Paralympic Participations
Biryla has participated in the following Paralympic events:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics**
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**
