# ZHAO Zhiqing: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
ZHAO Zhiqing is a Chinese athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW5/7 classification for standing athletes, ZHAO participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics held in Beijing.

## Career Overview
ZHAO Zhiqing's career in winter sports includes participation in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. She competed in various events at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 3rd place in Women's Sprint Standing at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
  - 2nd place in Women's Individual Standing at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
  
- **Biathlon:**
  - Silver medal in Women's 12.5 km Standing Biathlon at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
  - Bronze medal in Women's 6 km Standing Biathlon at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Performance Trends
- ZHAO achieved podium finishes in sprint events and strong placements in long-distance events during the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2022 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Cross-Country Skiing:
    - Women's Sprint Standing: 3rd place (March 5, 2022)
    - Women's Long Distance Classic Standing: 4th place (March 7, 2022)
    - Women's Sprint Free Standing: 4th place (March 9, 2022)
    - Women's Individual Standing: 2nd place (March 11, 2022)
  - Biathlon:
    - Women's 12.5 km Standing: Silver medal (March 12, 2022)
    - Women's 6 km Standing: Bronze medal (March 13, 2022)

## Other Sports
