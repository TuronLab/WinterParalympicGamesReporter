# EHLER Alexander: A Journey Through Paralympic Cross Country Skiing

## Introduction
Alexander Ehler is an athlete from Germany, competing in the sport of Cross Country Skiing, specifically in the Para Nordic Skiing category. He is classified under LW4. Ehler made his mark in the Paralympic arena following a motorcycle accident.

## Career Overview
Ehler's career in competitive skiing began at the age of nine. A motorcycle accident led to the amputation of part of his leg. After a hiatus from competitive sports, he resumed skiing in 2016, focusing on both Cross Country Skiing and Biathlon. His debut at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympics marked the beginning of his competitive journey.

## Major Achievements
- **Bronze Medal:** Men's Long Distance Sitting at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympics.
- **Bronze Medal:** Men's Long Distance Sitting at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
- **World Cup Medal:** Achieved his first World Cup medal, finishing third in a recent competition.

## Performance Trends
- Ehler has demonstrated consistent performance in various competitions, particularly noted for his bronze medal achievement at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympics.
- His participation in the 2022 Beijing Paralympics earned him another bronze medal in the Men's Long Distance Sitting event.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 PyeongChang Paralympics:**
  - Sport: Cross Country Skiing
  - Event: Men's Long Distance Sitting
  - Result: Bronze Medal
- **2022 Beijing Paralympics:**
  - Sport: Cross Country Skiing
  - Event: Men's Long Distance Sitting
  - Result: Bronze Medal
  - Event: Men's Sprint Free Standing
  - Result: Did not advance past the semifinals

## Other Sports
- **Biathlon:**
  - Competed in Biathlon events at the 2018 PyeongChang Paralympics but did not secure medals.

## Achievements in Other Sports
- **PyeongChang 2018:** Competed in Biathlon events.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Ehler's training regimen has been adapted to accommodate the challenges posed by his physical condition following his accident. Since resuming competitive skiing in 2016, he has focused on developing his skills in both Cross Country Skiing and Biathlon.

## Notable Facts
- Ehler began skiing at a young age and had aspirations of Olympic competition prior to his accident.
