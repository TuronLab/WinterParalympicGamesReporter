# Nico Messinger: A Rising Star in Cross Country Skiing

## Introduction
Nico Messinger is a visually impaired athlete representing Germany in the sport of Cross Country Skiing.

## Career Overview
Nico Messinger's career in cross country skiing has seen significant participation in international events, with a strong focus on the biathlon category. His journey includes:

- Active participation in both Cross Country Skiing and Biathlon events.
- A consistent presence in competitions leading up to the 2026 Paralympics.

## Major Achievements
Nico Messinger has gathered numerous accolades throughout his career. His major achievements include:

- **World Para Nordic Skiing Championships 2023**
  - **Event**: 10 km Biathlon  
  - **Achievement**: Silver Medal  
  - **Source**: [DRS.org](https://www.drs.org)

- **Silvester Race Notschrei, December 31, 2023**
  - **Overall Position**: 15th out of 19 participants  
  - **Source**: [RaceSplitter](https://www.racesplitter.com)

- Participation in multiple Para World Cup events.  
  - **Source**: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany_at_the_2022_Winter_Paralympics)

## Performance Trends
Nico Messinger has exhibited the following trends:

- Consistent placement among the top ranks in international competitions.
- Improvement leading toward the upcoming 2026 Paralympics.  
  - **Source**: [Team Deutschland Paralympics](https://www.teamdeutschlandparalympics.de)

## Paralympic Participations
Nico Messinger has made notable appearances in the Paralympics:

- **2018 PyeongChang Winter Paralympics**
  - **Sport**: Cross Country Skiing  
  - **Result**: Competed  
  - **Source**: [Team Deutschland Paralympics](https://www.teamdeutschlandparalympics.de)

- **2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics**
  - **Sport**: Cross Country Skiing  
  - **Result**: Competed  
  - **Source**: [Team Deutschland Paralympics](https://www.teamdeutschlandparalympics.de)

- **Upcoming**: Expected to participate in the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Paralympics.
