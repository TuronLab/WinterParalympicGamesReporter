# BEREZIN Yuriy: A Profile of a Paralympic Biathlete

## Introduction
Yuriy Berezin is a Paralympic athlete from Kazakhstan, specializing in biathlon and competing in the Sitting category. He represented his country at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Yuriy Berezin's career in winter sports includes competing at the highest level, particularly during the 2022 Beijing Paralympics, where he participated in multiple events in the Sitting category.

## Major Achievements
- **2022 Beijing Paralympics:**
  - Men's Long Distance Sitting: 24th place (2022-03-06)
  - Men's Sprint Sitting: 30th place (2022-03-09)
  - Men's Middle Distance Sitting: 31st place (2022-03-12)
- **Recent Competition:**
  - 13th place in the 7.5 km Sprint at the Paralympic Biathlon World Cup (source: [Qazinform](https://qazinform.com/news/yerbol-khamitov-hauls-bronze-at-paralympic-biathlon-world-cup-stage-a3197f))
