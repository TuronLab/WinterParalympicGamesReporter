## Maddie Mullin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

### Introduction
Maddie Mullin is a Canadian athlete competing in the sport of biathlon, specifically within the vision-impaired classification. Mullin is set to compete in the upcoming Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympics.

### Career Overview
Maddie Mullin began skiing at the age of ten. She competes in biathlon and also participates in Para cross-country skiing. She often competes alongside her guide, Brooke Ailey.

### Major Achievements
- **Biathlon:**
  - Finished second in three consecutive World Cup races in Canmore, Alberta.
  - Placed eighth in the women’s vision-impaired sprint at Notschrei, Germany.

### Performance Trends
- Recent competitions indicate a pattern of improving performance, including podium finishes.

### Paralympic Participations
- **2026 Milano Cortina Paralympics**
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Maddie will represent Canada.

### Other Sports
- **Para Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Competed in the Para Cross-Country World Cup.

### Preparation Style / Training Approach
Maddie emphasizes communication with her guide, Brooke Ailey, in her training approach.
