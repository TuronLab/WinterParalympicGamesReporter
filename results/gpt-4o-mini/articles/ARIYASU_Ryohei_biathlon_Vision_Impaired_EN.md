# Ryohei Ariyasu: Profile of a Paralympic Winter Sports Competitor

## Introduction  
Ryohei Ariyasu is an athlete from Japan, competing in the sports of Nordic skiing and rowing. Born on February 2, 1987, he primarily represents the visually impaired (VI) category in his sporting disciplines. Ariyasu has participated in significant international competitions, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview  
Ryohei Ariyasu’s career includes participation in Nordic skiing and rowing. His journey in elite sports encompasses:

- **Rowing**: Participated in the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.
- **Biathlon**: Competed in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games, representing Japan in events for visually impaired athletes.

## Major Achievements  
- **Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:  
  - Men's Long Distance Classic VI: 7th Place  
  - Men's Sprint Free VI: 16th Place (Qualification)  
  - Men's Middle Distance Free VI: 16th Place  
  *(Source: [International Paralympic Committee](https://www.paralympic.org/ryohei-ariyasu))*

- **Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games**:  
  - Rowing, PR3 Mixed Coxed Four:  
    - Heat 2: 6th Place (2021-08-27)  
    - Heat 2: 5th Place (2021-08-28)  
    - Final B: 6th Place (2021-08-29)  
  *(Source: [Paralympic Results](https://www.paralympic.org/ryohei-ariyasu))*
