# Liu Mengtao: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Liu Mengtao is a Chinese athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the LW12 classification for sitting athletes. Born on November 23, 2001, Liu participated in the 2022 Winter Paralympics held in Beijing.

## Career Overview
Liu Mengtao's career in winter sports includes participation in biathlon. He competed in the 2022 Winter Paralympics, marking his debut on the international stage. Liu is also involved in cross-country skiing, although specific achievements in this sport are not publicly available.

## Major Achievements
- **2022 Winter Paralympics (Beijing)**:
  - Gold Medal in Men's 6 km Sitting - Time: 30:37.7
  - Bronze Medal in Men's 12.5 km Sitting - Time: 19:33.3
- First Chinese Para biathlete to win two medals (gold and bronze) at a single Winter Paralympics.
