# Oksana Shyshkova: Paralympic Biathlon Star

## Introduction
Oksana Shyshkova is a notable Ukrainian athlete who competes in biathlon. Born on June 10, 1991, she is classified as visually impaired (B2) and has made significant contributions to her sport through performances in major international competitions, including the Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Oksana Shyshkova’s career has seen a steady ascent through the ranks of Paralympic sports, primarily focusing on biathlon. She debuted on the international stage at the 2010 Winter Paralympics, where she did not secure any medals. Her career progressed starting with the 2014 Winter Paralympics and continued through subsequent competitions. She has competed in both biathlon and cross-country skiing:
- **2010 Winter Paralympics:** Debut (no medals)
- **2014 Winter Paralympics:** 4 Medals (3 in biathlon, 1 in cross-country skiing)
- **2018 Winter Paralympics:** 5 Medals (3 gold, 2 silver)
- **2022 Winter Paralympics:** Achievements in biathlon events

## Major Achievements
- **2014 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Bronze Medal in 6km visually impaired biathlon
  - Bronze Medal in 10km visually impaired biathlon
  - Bronze Medal in 12.5km visually impaired biathlon
  - Bronze Medal in cross-country skiing
- **2018 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Gold Medal in Women's 10km visually impaired biathlon
  - Silver Medal in Women's 6km visually impaired biathlon
  - Bronze Medal in Women's cross-country mixed relay
- **2022 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Gold Medal in Women's 6km visually impaired biathlon
  - Achievements in 12.5 km and 10 km visually impaired biathlon
- **IPC World Championships 2017:**
  - 6 Medals in cross-country skiing events

## Performance Trends
- **2016-2017 Season:** Breakthrough season with multiple titles achieved.
- **2018-2019 Season:** Competitiveness in the World Cup Circuit.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2010 Winter Paralympics:** No medals
- **2014 Winter Paralympics:** 
  - 6km visually impaired biathlon: Bronze
  - 10km visually impaired biathlon: Bronze
  - 12.5km visually impaired biathlon: Bronze
  - Cross-country skiing: Bronze
- **2018 Winter Paralympics:** 
  - Women's 10km visually impaired biathlon: Gold
  - Women's 6km visually impaired biathlon: Silver
  - Women's cross-country mixed relay: Bronze
- **2022 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Women's 6km visually impaired biathlon: Gold
  - Events in 12.5 km and 10 km visually impaired races: Medals achieved

## Other Sports
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Consistently participated in events alongside biathlon
  - Achievements include multiple medals at world championships and Paralympic events
