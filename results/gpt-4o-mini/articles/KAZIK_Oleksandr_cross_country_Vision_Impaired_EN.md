# KAZIK Oleksandr: A One Man Army on Skis

## Introduction
Oleksandr Kazik is a Paralympic athlete from Ukraine, specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. He competes in the visually impaired category B1.

## Career Overview
Kazik's career in competitive sports primarily includes cross-country skiing and biathlon. Here's a chronological summary of his significant milestones:

- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Kazik competed in cross-country skiing with notable placements, including a silver medal in the Men's 12.5km event.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: He also participated in biathlon, securing a silver medal in the Men's 12.5km event.
- **2019 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**: He achieved placements, ranking 6th and 7th in the Men's Sprint and Middle Distance events respectively.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Kazik earned a gold medal in the Men's Individual VI category and a silver medal in the Men's Sprint VI.

## Major Achievements

### Cross-Country Skiing
- **Paralympic Medals**:
  - Silver in Men's 12.5km at the **2018 Winter Paralympics**
  - Gold in Men's Individual VI at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**
  - Silver in Men's Sprint VI at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**
- **World Championships**:
  - Bronze in Men's Sprint VI at the **Lillehammer 2021 Championships**

### Biathlon
- Silver in Men's 12.5km at the **2018 Winter Paralympics**
- Gold in Men's 12.5km at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**
- Silver in Men's 6km at the **2022 Winter Paralympics**

  - Cross-Country Skiing:
