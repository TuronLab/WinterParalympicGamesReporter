# Darya Fedzkovich: A Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Darya Fedzkovich is a cross-country skier from Belarus, born on October 3, 2000. Competing in the standing category, she has represented her country in significant international events, including the Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Darya Fedzkovich's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by her participation in key events. She made her Paralympic debut at the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games, where she competed in two events. Following this, she participated in the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships, where she competed in multiple distances.
