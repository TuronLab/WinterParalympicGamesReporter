# NITTA Yoshihiro: A Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing Icon

## Introduction
NITTA Yoshihiro (新田佳浩) is an athlete from Japan, specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Competing in the LW6/8 classification, Nitta has participated in seven consecutive Winter Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Nitta's career in competitive skiing began at a young age, following an accident that resulted in the loss of his left arm below the elbow when he was three years old. He took up skiing at the age of nine. His career trajectory in cross-country skiing is as follows:

- **1998 Nagano Paralympics**: 9th in Men's 7.5 km LW6/8
- **2002 Salt Lake City Paralympics**: Bronze in Men's 5 km Classical Technique LW5-9
- **2006 Torino Paralympics**: 5th in Men's 20 km Standing
- **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: 
  - Gold in Men's 1 km Sprint Standing
  - Gold in Men's 10 km Standing
- **2014 Sochi Paralympics**: 4th in Men's 20 km Standing
- **2018 Pyeongchang Paralympics**: 
  - Gold in Men's 10 km Classic Standing
  - Silver in Men's 1.5 km Sprint Classic Standing
- **2022 Beijing Paralympics**: 8th in Men's Sprint Free Standing

## Major Achievements
Nitta's accomplishments in cross-country skiing include:

- **Total Paralympic Medals**: 4 (2 Gold, 1 Silver, 1 Bronze)
- **Gold Medals**: 
  - 2010 Vancouver: 1 km Sprint Standing
  - 2010 Vancouver: 10 km Standing
  - 2018 Pyeongchang: 10 km Classic Standing
- **Silver Medal**: 
  - 2018 Pyeongchang: 1.5 km Sprint Classic Standing
- **Bronze Medal**: 
  - 2002 Salt Lake City: 5 km Classical Technique LW5-9

## Paralympic Participations
Nitta's participation in the Paralympic Games is as follows:

- **1998 Nagano Paralympics**: 9th in Men's 7.5 km LW6/8
- **2002 Salt Lake City Paralympics**: Bronze in Men's 5 km Classical Technique LW5-9
- **2006 Torino Paralympics**: 5th in Men's 20 km Standing
- **2010 Vancouver Paralympics**: 
  - Gold in Men's 1 km Sprint Standing
  - Gold in Men's 10 km Standing
- **2014 Sochi Paralympics**: 4th in Men's 20 km Standing
- **2018 Pyeongchang Paralympics**: 
  - Gold in Men's 10 km Classic Standing
  - Silver in Men's 1.5 km Sprint Classic Standing

## Other Sports
In addition to cross-country skiing, Nitta has also competed in biathlon events.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Nitta's training regimen includes:

- Focused on training abroad, particularly on-snow training in New Zealand.
- Emphasis on diet and fitness as critical components of his preparation.
- Adaptation of training methods to recover from injuries, such as incorporating cycling after an ankle sprain.
