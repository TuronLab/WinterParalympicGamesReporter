# Karl Tabouret: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Karl Tabouret is a 22-year-old French athlete specializing in cross-country skiing, competing in the LW3 classification for standing athletes. He represents the Albertville Handisport club and is selected for the 2026 Paralympic Games in Milan-Cortina.

## Career Overview
- **2025 World Championships (Trondheim, Norway)**: Achieved significant success with a gold medal in the sprint event and a 14th place finish in the individual skate.
- **2026 World Cup (Finsterau, Germany)**: Achieved a 2nd place finish in the interval start, a 5th place finish in the sprint final, a 7th place finish in the sprint qualification, and a 7th place finish in the open relay.

## Major Achievements
- **2025 World Championships**: 
  - Gold medal in the sprint event.
- **2026 World Cup**: 
  - 2nd place in the interval start.
  - 5th place in the sprint final.
  - 7th place in both the sprint qualification and open relay.

## Paralympic Participations
