# Joshua Sweeney: A Journey Through Paralympic Sports

## Introduction
Joshua Sweeney is an athlete from the United States, primarily competing in biathlon. He is classified under the Para category, focusing on the upcoming Milan/Cortina 2026 Paralympics. 

## Career Overview
Joshua Sweeney's athletic career has evolved significantly over the years:
- **Para Ice Hockey**: Sweeney began his Paralympic journey in ice hockey, where he achieved notable success.
- **Para Nordic Skiing**: He competed in the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
- **Biathlon**: Currently, Sweeney is focusing on biathlon, having transitioned from Para Nordic skiing.

## Major Achievements
- **Para Ice Hockey**: 
  - Gold medalist at the 2014 Sochi Paralympics.
- **Para Nordic Skiing**: 
  - Finished 16th in the 18 km sitting event at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
- **Triathlons**: 
  - Winner of the Para Development category at Leon's Triathlon.
  - Competed in Ironman 70.3 Boise.
