## SUIARKO Dmytro: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

### Introduction
SUIARKO Dmytro is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon, competing in the visually impaired category.

### Career Overview
Dmytro's career began in his native Ukraine. His journey to international recognition included participation in the 2014, 2018, and 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. Over the years, he has showcased his talent in various international competitions, steadily progressing in performance and achieving notable results:

- **2014 Paralympic Winter Games**: First appearance at the Paralympics.
- **2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: Competed in several events with strong finishes.
- **2022 Paralympic Winter Games**: Achieved significant podium finishes in both cross-country skiing and biathlon.

### Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - Bronze medal in men's 15 km visually impaired at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics (March 5, 2022).
  - Bronze medal in men's sprint at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics (March 9, 2022).
  - Consistent top-ten finishes, including 6th place in various events at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

- **Biathlon**:
  - Bronze medal in vision impaired middle distance at the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
  - 6th place in men's 15 km visually impaired at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

### Paralympic Participations
- **2014 Paralympic Winter Games**: Competed in cross-country skiing and biathlon.
- **2018 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Men's 10 km Classic - Visually impaired: 6th place.
  - Men's 15 km - Visually impaired: 6th place.
  - Men's Sprint Classic 1.5 km - Malvoyants: 6th place.
  - Men's 12.5 km - Visually impaired: 9th place.
  - Men's 7.5 km - Visually impaired: 6th place.
- **2022 Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Men's 15 km visually impaired: Bronze medal.
  - Men's sprint: Bronze medal.
  - Biathlon vision impaired middle distance: Bronze medal.

### Other Sports Participation
- **Biathlon**:
  - Bronze medalist at the 2022 Beijing Olympics in the vision impaired category.
  - Achieved 6th place in the men's 15 km visually impaired at the 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.

### Training Style / Preparation Approach
Dmytro's training is supported by a strong familial foundation, with notable influence from his father, who once coached him.

### Notable Facts
- **Date of Birth**: 22 October 1996.
- SUIARKO grew up in a Ukrainian village.
