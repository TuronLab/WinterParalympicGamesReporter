# Luca Tavasci: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Luca Tavasci is a Swiss Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. He competes in the LW8 classification. Tavasci has participated in two Paralympic Games and plans to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Luca Tavasci's career in cross-country skiing began with his participation in the 2018 Paralympic Games held in PyeongChang, South Korea. He has consistently competed in major international events, including the World Championships.

## Major Achievements
- **Paralympic Games:**
  - PyeongChang 2018: 
    - 11th place in Men's 20 km Classic - Standing
    - 14th place in Men's 10 km Freestyle - Standing
    - 16th place in Men's 1.5 km Sprint Classic - Standing
  - Beijing 2022:
    - 11th place in Men's Long Distance Classic - Standing
    - 13th place in Men's 12.5 km Freestyle - Standing
    - 16th place in Men's Sprint Freestyle - Standing
- **World Championships:**
  - 2023: 7th place in Men's 18 km Classic - Standing
  - 2022: 12th place in Men's Sprint Freestyle - Standing

## Performance Trends
- Consistent participation in major events since 2018.
- Improvement noted with a 7th place finish at the World Championships 2023.

## Paralympic Participations
- **PyeongChang 2018:**
  - Men's 20 km Classic - Standing: 11th place
  - Men's 10 km Freestyle - Standing: 14th place
  - Men's 1.5 km Sprint Classic - Standing: 16th place
- **Beijing 2022:**
  - Men's Long Distance Classic - Standing: 11th place
  - Men's 12.5 km Freestyle - Standing: 13th place
  - Men's Sprint Freestyle - Standing: 16th place

## Other Sports
- **Biathlon:**
  - Tavasci has participated in biathlon events and will compete in both biathlon and cross-country at the 2026 Paralympic Games.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Luca Tavasci trains primarily in St. Moritz, Switzerland, focusing on cross-country skiing and mountain biking during the off-season.

## Notable Facts
- Born on September 21, 1993, in Samedan, Switzerland.
- Overcame significant health challenges, including lymphoma and meningitis, before pursuing a career in sports.
- Currently a student in civil engineering.
