# Theo BOLD: A Promising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Theo BOLD is a German athlete competing in Cross-Country Skiing under the category of Men VI (Visually Impaired). He is a member of Team Deutschland Paralympics and is scheduled to compete in the upcoming Paralympic Games in Milano Cortina 2026. He is recognized as an athlete to watch in upcoming competitions.

## Career Overview
Theo BOLD's career includes participation in Cross-Country Skiing and Biathlon. 

- **Cross-Country Skiing**: Competing in the Men VI category, Theo is gearing up for the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games.
