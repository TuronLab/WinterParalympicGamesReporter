# Liu Xiaobin: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
Liu Xiaobin is a Chinese athlete competing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Born on September 19, 2000, he is currently 25 years old and classified under the LW5/7 category for standing athletes.

## Career Overview
Liu Xiaobin's career in winter sports has been marked by a focus on cross-country skiing and biathlon. His journey includes:

- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Achieved success in both biathlon and cross-country skiing.
- **2024-2026**: Continued participation in World Cup events.

## Major Achievements
Liu Xiaobin has garnered several accolades throughout his career, including:

- **Gold Medal**: Men's 12.5 km Visually Impaired Biathlon at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- **Gold Medal**: Cross-country skiing events at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.
- **Podium Finishes**: Multiple top-10 finishes in World Cup events, including:
  - 2nd place in Mass Start at Vuokatti, Finland (December 20, 2024).
  - 2nd place in Interval Start at Toblach, Italy (February 14, 2025).
  - 3rd place in Open Relay at Finsterau, Germany (January 18, 2026).

## Paralympic Participations
Liu Xiaobin has participated in the following Paralympic events:

- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Gold medal in Men's 12.5 km Visually Impaired Biathlon.
  - Gold medal in cross-country skiing events.

## Other Sports
Liu primarily focuses on cross-country skiing and biathlon, with achievements including:

- **Gold Medal**: Biathlon events at the 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Liu Xiaobin's training regimen emphasizes endurance and technique, with a strong focus on both cross-country skiing and biathlon skills. He trains under the guidance of national team coaches.

## Notable Facts
- Liu Xiaobin competes in the standing category, which means he does not require a guide.
