### Thomas Oxaal: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

#### Introduction
Thomas Karbøl Oxaal is a Norwegian athlete born on October 21, 1999, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the B3 category for visually impaired athletes.

#### Career Overview
Thomas Oxaal’s career in cross-country skiing has seen him participate in international competitions primarily within the LW10 classification for visually impaired athletes. His timeline includes significant events starting with the World Para Nordic Skiing Championships in 2019 and culminating at the 2022 Winter Paralympics in Beijing.

#### Major Achievements
- **Beijing 2022 Winter Paralympics:**
  - Silver Medal in Men's 4x2.5 km Open Relay (Position: 2nd)
  - 9th Place in Men's Middle Distance Free VI (Position: 9th) [March 12, 2022]
  - 12th Place in Men's Sprint Free VI (Qualification)
  - 3rd Place in Open 4x2.5 km Relay [March 13, 2022]
  
- **World Para Nordic Skiing Championships (2019, Prince George):**
  - 13th Place in Men's Middle Distance - Free Style VI [February 17, 2019]
  - 6th Place in Men's Sprint - Free Style VI [February 18, 2019]
  - 8th Place in Men's Long Distance - Classic VI [February 24, 2019]
