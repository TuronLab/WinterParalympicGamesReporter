# Simona Bubenickova: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing 

## Introduction
Simona Bubenickova, an accomplished athlete representing Czechia, was born in 2008 and competes in the NS1 classification for athletes with vision impairments.

## Career Overview
Simona Bubenickova has rapidly ascended in the world of parasports, particularly in cross-country skiing. She has consistently participated in major competitions, achieving notable success. She competes in both cross-country skiing and biathlon.

## Major Achievements
- **Gold medals in Cross-Country:**
  - 10 km classic at the 2025 Para Cross-Country World Championships.
  - 5 km free style at the 2025 Para Cross-Country World Championships.
- **Podium Finishes:**
  - Consistently on the podium in World Cup events, achieving third place in Women VI during the 7.5 km sprint.
