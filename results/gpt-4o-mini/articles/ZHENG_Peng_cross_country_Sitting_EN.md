# ZHENG Peng: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
ZHENG Peng, born on October 21, 1992, is an athlete from China, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the LW10 disability category. ZHENG has participated in the 2018 and 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
ZHENG Peng began his journey in cross-country skiing in 2016. He became the first Chinese Para skier to win a silver medal at a World Cup event in Canada in 2017. His participation in the Paralympic Games began in 2018.

## Major Achievements
- **Gold Medals**:
  - 18 km sitting event at the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.
  - 1.5 km sprint sitting event at the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.
- **Silver Medals**:
  - 10 km sitting event at the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.
  - 4 × 2.5 km mixed relay at the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.
- **Notable Recognition**:
  - First Chinese athlete to win a gold medal in Paralympic cross-country skiing.
  - Most decorated Chinese winter Paralympian with a total of four medals at the 2022 Games.

## Performance Trends
- ZHENG improved from a 4th place finish in the 15 km sitting event at the 2018 PyeongChang Winter Paralympics to winning two gold medals and two silver medals at the 2022 Beijing Winter Paralympics.

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 PyeongChang Winter Paralympics**:
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing
