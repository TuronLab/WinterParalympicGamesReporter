# Wesley Vinicius DOS SANTOS: A Rising Star in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Wesley Vinicius DOS SANTOS is a Brazilian athlete competing in the sport of biathlon, specifically in the sitting category. He is classified under Sport Class LW11.5 (SI - Sitting). DOS SANTOS competed in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Wesley Vinicius DOS SANTOS's career in biathlon is highlighted by his participation in the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games. In addition to biathlon, he is also involved in Nordic skiing.
