# Louise Perottosdotter: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Louise Perottosdotter is a Swedish para-athlete specializing in cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW9 classification (Standing), she is currently preparing for the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games. Her journey in para-sport began after high school.

## Career Overview
Louise Perottosdotter's career in cross-country skiing has been marked by consistent participation in World Cup events. Her competitive journey includes:

- **2022**: Achieved a podium finish in the World Cup season.
- **2026**: Competed in multiple events at the Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup.

## Major Achievements
- **1st place** in the 2022 World Cup season (Cross-Country).
- **5th place** in Mass Start at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup on 01-02-2026 (Cross-Country).
- **7th place** in Interval Start at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup on 31-01-2026 (Cross-Country).
- **8th place** in Interval Start at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup on 29-01-2026 (Cross-Country).
- **11th place** in Sprint Qualification at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup on 28-01-2026 (Cross-Country).
- **11th place** in Sprint Final at Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce World Cup on 28-01-2026 (Cross-Country).
- Awarded "Årets idrottspristagare" (Athlete of the Year) in Malung-Sälen for 2022.

## Performance Trends
- Consistently placed within the top 15 in World Cup events.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Preparing for the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games** (Sport: Cross-Country).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Louise Perottosdotter engages in cycling, running, roller skiing, and strength training during the off-season. She trains individually and occasionally with her coach and the national team.

## Notable Facts
