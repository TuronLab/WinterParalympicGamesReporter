# Karl Tabouret: Rising Star in Para Biathlon

## Introduction
Karl Tabouret is a 22-year-old French para biathlete, born on May 3, 2003, in Albertville, Savoie, France. Competing in the LW3 classification (standing), he is a member of the Albertville Handisport club and participates in para Nordic skiing, which includes biathlon.

## Career Overview
Karl Tabouret began his journey in para Nordic skiing at the age of 8. He was selected by the French Handisport team at the age of 17. 

## Major Achievements
- **2025 World Championships, Trondheim:**
  - Gold Medal in Sprint Classic (Standing)
  - Silver Medal in 10km Classic (Standing)
- **2024 World Championships, Prince George:**
  - 7th in Sprint Pursuit (Standing)
  - 11th in Biathlon 12.5km (Standing)
- **2020 European Youth Paralympic Games, Poland:**
  - Three Gold Medals (Biathlon, Sprint, Classic)

## Performance Trends
- **2024 Season:**
  - 1st in Sprint Classic and 1st in 5km Free at Beitostoelen, Norway.
- **2025 Season:**
  - Continued success with podium finishes in World Cup events.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Upcoming Participation:**
  - Selected for the Milan-Cortina 2026 Paralympic Games.

## Other Sports
- **Other Sports:** Competes in Para Cross-Country Skiing.
- **Achievements in Other Sports:**
  - Gold Medal in Sprint Classic and 5km Free at Beitostoelen (2024).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Karl Tabouret's training is guided by coaches Vincent Duchene and Anaïs Bescond, who focus on both Nordic skiing and biathlon.
