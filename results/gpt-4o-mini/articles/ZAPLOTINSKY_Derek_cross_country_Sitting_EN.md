# Derek Zaplotinsky: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Derek Zaplotinsky is a Canadian athlete competing in cross-country skiing, specifically in the men's sitting category (LW10.5). 

## Career Overview
Derek Zaplotinsky began his athletic career in cycling before transitioning to cross-country skiing. Since 2018, he has consistently participated in major events. 

## Major Achievements
- **1st place** in the men's sitting 5 km at the Para Nordic World Cup (2024) [Source: CBC Sports](https://www.cbc.ca/sports/paralympics/winter-sports/para-world-cup-zaplotinsky-cross-country-1.7144043).
- **8th place** in the middle-distance event at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Games [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).
- Competed in two Paralympic Games: 2018 and 2022 [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).

## Performance Trends
- Achieved his first World Cup title in 2024 [Source: CBC Sports](https://www.cbc.ca/sports/paralympics/winter-sports/para-world-cup-zaplotinsky-cross-country-1.7144043).
- Consistent participation in major events since 2018 [Source: Paralympic Canada](https://paralympic.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 Winter Paralympic Games**: Competed in cross-country skiing [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).
- **2022 Winter Paralympic Games**: 8th place in the middle-distance event [Source: Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/derek-zaplotinsky/).

## Other Sports
