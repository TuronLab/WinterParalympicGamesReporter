# Kendall Gretsch: A Trailblazer in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Kendall Gretsch is an accomplished American athlete specializing in biathlon, competing in the Sitting category. Born on April 2, 1992, in Downers Grove, Illinois, Gretsch has made significant strides in the world of Paralympic sports, particularly in biathlon and paratriathlon.

## Career Overview
Kendall Gretsch's career in competitive sports began with her involvement in paratriathlon before she transitioned to biathlon. 

- **2018**: Made her Paralympic debut at the PyeongChang Winter Games, where she achieved remarkable success in biathlon.
- **2020**: Competed in the Tokyo Summer Games, securing a gold medal in paratriathlon.
- **2022**: Returned to the Winter Paralympics in Beijing, where she continued to excel in biathlon events.
- **2024**: Scheduled to participate in the upcoming Paralympic Games.

## Major Achievements
- **Biathlon**:
  - Gold medal in Women's 6km Sitting at the PyeongChang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
  - Gold medal in Women's 12km Sitting at the PyeongChang 2018 Paralympic Winter Games.
  - Gold medal in 10km Middle - Sitting - Women at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
  - Silver medal in 12.5km Individual - Sitting - Women at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
  - Bronze medal in 6km Individual - Sitting - Women at the Beijing 2022 Paralympic Winter Games.
  - First female athlete from the USA to win a gold medal in biathlon.

- **Paratriathlon**:
  - Gold medal at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.
  - Multiple world championships in paratriathlon from 2014 to 2016.

## Performance Trends
- Gretsch has demonstrated consistent improvement and success since her debut in 2018, winning multiple medals in subsequent competitions, including the 2022 Paralympics.

## Paralympic Participations
- **PyeongChang 2018**:
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events: Women's 6km Sitting (Gold), Women's 12km Sitting (Gold)
  
- **Tokyo 2020**:
  - Sport: Paratriathlon

- **Beijing 2022**:
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events: 10km Middle - Sitting - Women (Gold), 12.5km Individual - Sitting - Women (Silver), 6km Individual - Sitting - Women (Bronze)

- **2024**: Scheduled to participate in the upcoming Paralympic Games.

## Other Sports
- **Paratriathlon**:
  - Gold medal at the Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games.
  - Multiple world championships in paratriathlon (2014-2016).

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Kendall Gretsch focuses on both skiing efficiency and shooting accuracy in her training regimen.
