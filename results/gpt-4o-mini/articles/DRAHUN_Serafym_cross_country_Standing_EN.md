# DRAHUN Serafym: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Serafym Drahun is a 20-year-old male athlete from Ukraine, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the LW8 category, which is designated for standing athletes.

## Career Overview
Serafym Drahun's career in cross-country skiing includes:

- **2021**: Competed in the Lillehammer World Para Snow Sports Championships, where he participated in multiple events.
- **2022**: Competed in the Beijing Paralympic Winter Games.

## Major Achievements
Drahun has achieved notable success in his sporting career, including:

- **Big Crystal Globe Winner**: FIS Para Cross-Country for the 2025/26 season in the Standing category.
