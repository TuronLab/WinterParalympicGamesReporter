# Arkadz Shykuts: A Profile of a Paralympic Cross-Country Athlete

## Introduction
Arkadz Shykuts is a Belarusian athlete competing in cross-country skiing, specifically in the sitting classification (LW12). Born on October 8, 1986, Shykuts has participated in the Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Arkadz Shykuts has participated in various events since 2018. He competes in the following categories:
- 7.5 km Sitting
- Sprint 1.1 km Sitting

## Major Achievements
- **2018 Paralympic Winter Games**: 
  - 22nd place in Men's 7.5 km Sitting (17 March 2018) [Source: The-Sports.org]
  - 19th place in Men's Sprint 1.1 km Sitting (14 March 2018) [Source: The-Sports.org]
- **Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**:
  - 3rd place in Mixed Relay (23 January 2022) [Source: Paralympic.org]
  - 15th place in Men's Sprint Sitting (15 January 2022) [Source: Paralympic.org]
  - 14th place in Men's Middle Distance Sitting (16 January 2022) [Source: Paralympic.org]
  - 11th place in Men's Long Distance Sitting (20 January 2022) [Source: Paralympic.org]

## Paralympic Participations
- **2018 Pyeongchang Paralympic Winter Games**:
  - Men's 7.5 km Sitting: 22nd place (17 March 2018) [Source: The-Sports.org]
