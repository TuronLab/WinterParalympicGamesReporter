# Stefan Egger-Riedmueller: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Stefan Egger-Riedmueller is an Austrian Paralympic athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and para biathlon. Competing in the LW4 sport class (standing), he has participated in the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympics and is set to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Stefan Egger-Riedmueller began his skiing journey five years ago, inspired by his cousin. He has a congenital condition (fibular hemimelia) resulting in the absence of the right fibula and associated musculature.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - Bronze medal at the Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympics.
  - 2x Gold and 1x Silver at the World Championships in Prince George (CAN) 2019.
  - 2x Gold and 1x Silver at the World Championships in Finsterau (GER) 2017.
  - Overall World Cup winner in the 2016/2017 and 2017/2018 seasons.
  - 3rd place in the overall standings of the Para Cross-Country World Cup 2026.

## Performance Trends
- Achieved three Top Ten placements at the World Cup event in Szklarska Poreba-Jakuszyce (POL).
- Six Top Ten results in the 2025/2026 season.

## Paralympic Participations
- **Pyeongchang 2018 Paralympics:**
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing
  - Result: Bronze medal
- **Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympics:**
  - Sport: Cross-Country Skiing

## Other Sports
