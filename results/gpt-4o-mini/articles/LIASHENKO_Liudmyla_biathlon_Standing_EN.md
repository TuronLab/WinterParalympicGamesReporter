# Liudmyla Liashenko: A Beacon of Resilience in Paralympic Biathlon

## Introduction
Liudmyla Liashenko is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon and cross-country skiing. Competing in the LW6/8 classification, she has participated in the Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
Liudmyla Liashenko's career in biathlon includes:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: First appearance in the Paralympic Games.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: Achieved her first medal in biathlon.
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: Secured multiple medals.

## Major Achievements
Liudmyla Liashenko's achievements in biathlon include:

- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Bronze in Women's 6 km Standing.
  
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Gold in Women's Individual Standing.
  - Silver in Women's Sprint Standing.
  - Bronze in Women's Middle Distance Standing.

Additionally, she has earned multiple medals at the World Para Snow Sports Championships.

## Performance Trends
Liudmyla's performance trends include:

- Consistent medal performance across the 2018 and 2022 Winter Paralympics.

## Paralympic Participations
Liudmyla Liashenko has participated in the following Winter Paralympics:

- **2014 Winter Paralympics**: Competed in biathlon.
- **2018 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Event: Women's 6 km Standing
  - Result: Bronze
- **2022 Winter Paralympics**: 
  - Sport: Biathlon
  - Events: 
    - Women's Individual Standing - Gold
    - Women's Sprint Standing - Silver
    - Women's Middle Distance Standing - Bronze

## Other Sports
Liudmyla has also competed in cross-country skiing:

- **2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships**: 
  - Bronze in Women's 10 km Standing.

## Preparation Style / Training Approach
Liudmyla Liashenko emphasizes a rigorous training regimen.

## Notable Facts
- Born on May 17, 1993, in Ukraine.
