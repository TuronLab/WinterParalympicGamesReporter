# Witold Skupien: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Witold Skupien is a Polish cross-country skier competing in the LW5/7 classification for standing athletes. Born on June 7, 1989, he is currently 36 years old and remains an active competitor in the sport. Skupien is scheduled to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games, following his previous participation in the 2022 Beijing Paralympics.
