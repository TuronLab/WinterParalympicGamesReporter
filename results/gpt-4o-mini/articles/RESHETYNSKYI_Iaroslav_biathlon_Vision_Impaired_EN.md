# Athlete Profile: Iaroslav Reshetynskyi

## Introduction  
Iaroslav Reshetynskyi is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in biathlon and Nordic skiing. Born on September 24, 1992, he competes in the visually impaired (VI) classification.

## Career Overview  
Iaroslav Reshetynskyi's career trajectory reflects a focused commitment to competing in winter sports, particularly in biathlon and Nordic skiing:

- **2014 Sochi Paralympic Games**: Participated in multiple events.
- **2018 Pyeongchang Paralympic Games**: Participated in five events.
- **2019 World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**: Captured a bronze medal in the Men's Long Distance VI.
- **2022 Beijing Paralympic Games**: Competed in four events.

## Major Achievements  
- **World Para Nordic Skiing Championships**: 
  - Bronze in Men's Long Distance VI (2019)
- **Paralympic Games**:  
  - 4th place in Men's 7.5 km VI (Pyeongchang 2018)
  - 4th place in Men's Middle Distance VI (Beijing 2022)
  - 5th place in Men's 10 km Classic VI (Pyeongchang 2018)
  - 6th place in Men's Sprint VI (Beijing 2022)
