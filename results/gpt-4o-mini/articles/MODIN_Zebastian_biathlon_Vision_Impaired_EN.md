### Introduction  
Zebastian Tintin Modin, born on June 20, 1994, is a Swedish athlete known for his achievements in biathlon and cross-country skiing. Competing in the B1 classification due to visual impairment, Modin has represented Sweden in three Paralympic Games.

### Career Overview  
Zebastian Modin has participated predominantly in biathlon and also achieved results in cross-country skiing. He is affiliated with Östersunds SK for biathlon and Synskadades IF Träffen.

### Major Achievements  
- **Paralympic Games:**
  - **2010 Vancouver:**
    - Bronze Medal - Men's 1 km Sprint, Visually Impaired
  - **2014 Sochi:**
    - Silver Medal - Men's 1 km Freestyle Sprint
    - Silver Medal - Men's 4x2.5 km Mixed Relay
    - 4th Place - Men's 20 km Classical
  - **2018 Pyeongchang:**
    - Silver Medal - Men's 1.5 km Sprint Classical
    - 4th Place - Men's 20 km Free
    - 7th Place - Men's 7.5 km
- **Other Results:**
  - 9th Place - Men's 10 km, Visually Impaired (2018)
  - 11th Place - Men's 20 km, Visually Impaired (2018)
