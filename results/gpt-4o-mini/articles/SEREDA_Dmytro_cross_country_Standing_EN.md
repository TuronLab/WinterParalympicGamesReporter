# Dmytro Sereda: A Rising Star in Paralympic Winter Sports

## Introduction
Dmytro Sereda is a Ukrainian athlete specializing in cross-country skiing and biathlon. Born on February 8, 1998, he competes in the Nordic skiing category. Sereda is scheduled to compete in the Milano Cortina 2026 Paralympic Winter Games.

## Career Overview
Dmytro Sereda's career in winter sports includes participation in both cross-country skiing and biathlon. His notable achievements stem from the Lillehammer 2021 World Para Snow Sports Championships.
