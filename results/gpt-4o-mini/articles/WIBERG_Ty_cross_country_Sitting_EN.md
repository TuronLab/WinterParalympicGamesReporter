# Ty Wiberg: Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Ty Wiberg is a 24-year-old athlete from the United States, competing in cross-country skiing within the LW11.5 classification (sitting category). Wiberg is currently preparing for the 2026 Winter Paralympic Games in Milano Cortina.

## Career Overview
Ty Wiberg's career in competitive skiing began in 2018, where he has participated in various World Cup and Continental Cup events. In addition to cross-country skiing, Wiberg has also competed in para biathlon, alpine skiing, wheelchair racing, and holds a black belt in karate.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing**:
  - Competed in multiple World Cups and Continental Cups since 2018.
  - Selected for the U.S. Paralympics Nordic Skiing development team.
  - Aiming to qualify for the 2026 Winter Paralympic Games.

- **Para Biathlon**:
  - 21st place in the men's sitting 7.5-kilometer biathlon sprint at the World Cup in January 2025.
