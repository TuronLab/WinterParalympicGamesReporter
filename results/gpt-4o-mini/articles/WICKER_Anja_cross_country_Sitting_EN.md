# Anja Wicker: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Anja Wicker is a German athlete competing in cross-country skiing, classified under the LW10 category for sitting athletes. Born on December 22, 1991, Wicker has established herself as a competitor in the world of Paralympic sports, particularly in cross-country skiing and para biathlon.

## Career Overview
Anja Wicker's career has been marked by achievements in both cross-country skiing and para biathlon. 

- **Cross-Country Skiing:** Wicker has made strides in cross-country skiing, culminating in her success at the 2025 Para Cross-Country World Championships.
- **Para Biathlon:** Wicker has also competed in para biathlon.

## Major Achievements
- **Cross-Country Skiing:**
  - 2025 World Champion in the Sitting 10km Interval Start Classic (source: [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/para-cross-country/news/2024-25/wicker-mao-and-bubenickova-earn-maiden-titles-in-toblach))

- **Para Biathlon:**
  - 2024 Para Biathlon World Championships: Bronze medal in the Teamsprint (with Andrea Eskau) (source: [BSSA](https://nordski.de/DBS/BSSA))
