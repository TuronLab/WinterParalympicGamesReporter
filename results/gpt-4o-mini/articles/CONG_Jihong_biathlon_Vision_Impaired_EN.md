# Jihong Cong: Rising Star in Para Biathlon

## Introduction
CONG Jihong, born on April 25, 1999, is a female athlete representing China in the sport of Para Biathlon, classified within the NS3 category for vision impairments. Cong is set to participate in the upcoming 2026 Winter Paralympics in Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo, Italy. 

## Career Overview
Jihong Cong has been actively competing in Para Biathlon, showcasing her skills predominantly in the 2025/2026 season. She has also participated in Para Cross-Country Skiing.
