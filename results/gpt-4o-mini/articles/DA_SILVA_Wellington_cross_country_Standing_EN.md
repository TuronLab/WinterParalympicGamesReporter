# Wellington da Silva: Rising Star in Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Wellington da Silva is an athlete from Brazil, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the LW8 category, which is designated for standing athletes. Wellington is scheduled to make his debut at the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Paralympics.

## Career Overview
