# Liu Mengtao: A Rising Star in Paralympic Cross-Country Skiing

## Introduction
Liu Mengtao is a talented athlete from China, competing in cross-country skiing. He is classified under the LW12 category, which is designated for sitting athletes. Liu has participated in the prestigious Paralympic Games.

## Career Overview
Liu Mengtao's career in cross-country skiing includes:

- **2022**: Competed in the Winter Paralympics.
- **2024 Season**: Achieved a podium finish with a second-place result in one of the World Cup events.
- **2025 Season**: Secured two podium finishes, including one second place and one third place.
- **2026**: Participated in multiple World Cup events, including 11th in Sprint Qualification and 10th in Sprint Final at Finsterau.
